slug: hc-15

One who is a true and righteous man, and yet more powerful than all creatures; that is, one who is at the same time true God.

## Proofs
- One who is a true (1 Corinthians 15:21; Hebrews 2:17)
- and righteous (Isaiah 53:9; 2 Corinthians 5:21; Hebrews 7:26)
- man, and yet more powerful than all creatures; that is, one who is at the same time true God. (Isaiah 7:14; Isaiah 9:6; Jeremiah 23:6; John 1:1; Romans 8:3–4)
