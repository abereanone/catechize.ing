title: Seeing then by the just Judgment of God we are subject both to temporal and eternal Punishments, is there yet any way or means remaining whereby we may be delivered from these Punishments, and be reconciled to God?
slug: aoc-12
categories:
  - aoc
relatedAnswers:

God will have his Justice satisfied: wherefore it is necessary that we satisfy either by our selves, or by another.

## Proofs
- God will have his (Exodus 20:5–7; Exodus 23:7)
- Justice satisfied: wherefore it is necessary (Romans 8:3)
- that we satisfy either by our selves, or by another.
