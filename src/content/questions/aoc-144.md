title: Why is that added, which art in Heaven?
slug: aoc-144

  - aoc

That we conceive not basely nor terrenely of God’s Heavenly Majesty, and also that we look for, and expect from his Omnipotency whatsoever things are necessary for our Soul and Body.

## Proofs
- That we (Jeremiah 23:24; Acts 17:24–25,27)
- conceive not basely nor terrenely of God’s Heavenly Majesty, and also that we (Romans 10:12)
- look for, and expect from his Omnipotency whatsoever things are necessary for our Soul and Body.
