id: 36
title: What benefit do you receive from the holy conception and birth of Christ?
slug: hc-36
categories:
  - hc
---

He is our Mediator, and with His innocence and perfect holiness covers, in the sight of God, my sin, in which I was conceived and born.

## Proofs
- He is our Mediator, (1 Timothy 2:5–6; Hebrews 9:13–15)
- and with His innocence and perfect holiness covers, in the sight of God, my sin, in which I was conceived and born. (Romans 8:3–4; 2 Corinthians 5:21; Galatians 4:4–5; 1 Peter 1:18–19)
