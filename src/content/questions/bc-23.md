God having out of his mere good pleasure, from all eternity, elected some to everlasting life, did enter into a covenant of grace, to deliver them out of the estate of sin and misery, and to bring them into an estate of salvation by a Redeemer.

## Proofs
- God having out of his mere good pleasure, from all eternity, elected some to everlasting life, (Ephesians 1:4–5)
- did enter into a covenant of grace, to deliver them out of the estate of sin and misery, and to bring them into an estate of salvation by a Redeemer. (Romans 3:20–22; Galatians 3:21–22)
