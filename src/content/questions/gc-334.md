title: "Can you show that there is nothing inconsistent in this?"
slug: gc-334
categories:

---

Circumcision was also a sacrament of repentance, as Moses and the prophets declare (Deu 10:16; 30:6; Jer 4:4); and was a sacrament of faith, as St. Paul says (Rom 4:11, 12). And yet God has not excluded little children from it.
