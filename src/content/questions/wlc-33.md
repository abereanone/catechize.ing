slug: wlc-33

The covenant of grace was not always administered after the same manner, but the administrations of it under the Old Testament were different from those under the New.

## Proofs
- The covenant of grace was not always administered after the same manner, but the administrations of it under the Old Testament were different from those under the New. (2 Corinthians 3:6–9)
