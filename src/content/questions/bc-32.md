We are made partakers of the redemption purchased by Christ, by the effectual application of it to us by his Holy Spirit.

## Proofs
- We are made partakers of the redemption purchased by Christ, by the effectual application of it to us (John 1:11–12)
- by his Holy Spirit. (Titus 3:5–6)

## Additional Exposition

<a href="https://baptistcatechism.org/beddome/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">From Benjamin Beddome</a>


### Section 1

**Q:** Is there a redemption wrought out for the people of God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He sent redemption unto his people, (Psalm 111:9)

**Q:** Is Christ the author of this redemption?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He obtained eternal redemption for us, (Hebrews 9:12)

**Q:** Is his blood the price of it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** We are redeemed with the precious blood of Christ, (1 Peter 1:18–19)

**Q:** Do many expect redemption in another way?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Because I am innocent, surely his anger shall turn from me, (Jeremiah 2:35)

**Q:** But is this the only way?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For other foundation can no man lay, than that which is laid, which is Jesus Christ, (1 Corinthians 3:11)

### Section 2

**Q:** Do all partake of the redemption purchased by Christ?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** Some have neither part nor lot in this matter, (Acts 8:21)

**Q:** Do all partake of it who pretend to it?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** Thou hast a name that thou livest and art dead, (Revelation 3:1)

**Q:** Do all believers partake of it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** We are made partakers of Christ, (Hebrews 3:14)

**Q:** Do such receive the Redeemer?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** To as many as received him, gave he power to become the sons of God, (John 1:12)

**Q:** Do they receive him in all his offices?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ye have received Christ Jesus the Lord, (Colossians 2:6)

**Q:** And do any receive this of themselves?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** For a man can receive nothing, except it be given him from above, (John 3:27)

### Section 3

**Q:** Is it necessary that redemption be applied to us?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Who of God is made unto us redemption, (1 Corinthians 1:30)

**Q:** Is this a great and effectual work?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For there is an exceeding greatness of power manifested to us-ward who believe, (Ephesians 1:19)

**Q:** Is it a work within us?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** ’Tis Christ in us, the hope of glory, (Colossians 1:27)

**Q:** Is it the work of the Spirit?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** ’Tis the Spirit that quickens, (John 6:63)

**Q:** Is it wrought only upon the elect?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The election hath obtained it, (Romans 11:7)

**Q:** And shall all those be partakers of this redemption to whom it is effectually applied?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He that believeth on the Son hath everlasting life, (John 3:36)
