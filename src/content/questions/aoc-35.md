That he is our Mediator, and doth cover with his Innocency and perfect Holiness my Sins, in which I was conceived, that they may not come in the Sight of God.

## Proofs
- That he is our (Hebrews 2:16–17)
- Mediator, and doth cover with his Innocency and perfect Holiness my Sins, in which I was conceived, that they may not come in the Sight of (Psalm 32:1; 1 Corinthians 1:30; Romans 8:3–4; Galatians 4:4–5)
- God.
