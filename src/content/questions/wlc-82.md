The communion in glory which the members of the invisible church have with Christ, is in this life, immediately after death, and at last perfected at the resurrection and day of judgment.

## Proofs
- The communion in glory which the members of the invisible church have with Christ, is in this life, (2 Corinthians 3:18)
- immediately after death, (Luke 23:43)
- and at last perfected at the resurrection and day of judgment. (1 Thessalonians 4:17)
