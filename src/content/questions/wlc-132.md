The sins of equals are, besides the neglect of the duties required, the undervaluing of the worth, envying the gifts, grieving at the advancement of prosperity one of another; and usurping preeminence one over another.

## Proofs
- The sins of equals are, besides the neglect of the duties required, (Romans 13:8)
- the undervaluing of the worth, (2 Timothy 3:3)
- envying the gifts, (Acts 7:9; Galatians 5:26)
- grieving at the advancement of prosperity one of another; (Numbers 12:2; Esther 6:12–13)
- and usurping preeminence one over another. (3 John 9; Luke 22:24)
