Christ the Son of God became man, by taking to himself a true body, and a reasonable soul, being conceived by the power of the Holy Ghost in the womb of the virgin Mary, of her substance, and born of her, yet without sin.

## Proofs
- Christ the Son of God became man, by taking to himself a true body, and a reasonable soul, (John 1:14; Matthew 26:38)
- being conceived by the power of the Holy Ghost in the womb of the virgin Mary, of her substance, and born of her, (Luke 1:27, 31, 35, 42; Galatians 4:4)
- yet without sin. (Hebrews 4:15; 7:26)
