slug: hc-2

First, how great my sins and misery are; second, how I am delivered from all my sins and misery; third, how I am to be thankful to God for such deliverance.

## Proofs
- First, how great my sins and misery are; (Romans 3:9–10; 1 John 1:10)
- second, how I am delivered from all my sins and misery; (John 17:3; Acts 4:12; Acts 10:43)
- third, how I am to be thankful to God for such deliverance. (Matthew 5:16; Romans 6:13; Ephesians 5:8–10; 1 Peter 2:9–10)
