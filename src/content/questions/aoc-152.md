slug: aoc-152

That the thing is sure, and out of doubt: for my Prayer is much more certainly heard of God, than I feel in my Heart that I unfeignedly desire the same.

## Proofs
- That the thing is sure, and out of doubt: for my Prayer is much more certainly heard of God, than I feel in my Heart that I unfeignedly (2 Corinthians 1:20; 2 Timothy 2:13)
- desire the same.
