All that is promised us in the Gospel, which the articles of our catholic and undoubted Christian faith teach us in a summary.

## Proofs
- All that is promised us in the Gospel, (Matthew 28:19; John 20:30–31)
- which the articles of our catholic and undoubted Christian faith teach us in a summary.
