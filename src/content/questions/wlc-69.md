title: What is the communion in grace which the members of the invisible church have with Christ?
slug: wlc-69
categories:
  - wlc
---

The communion in grace which the members of the invisible church have with Christ, is their partaking of the virtue of his mediation, in their justification, adoption, sanctification, and whatever else, in this life, manifests their union with him.

## Proofs
- The communion in grace which the members of the invisible church have with Christ, is their partaking of the virtue of his mediation, in their justification, (Romans 8:30)
- adoption, (Ephesians 1:5)
- sanctification, and whatever else, in this life, manifests their union with him. (1 Corinthians 1:30)
