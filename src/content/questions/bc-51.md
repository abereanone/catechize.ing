The first commandment requireth us to know and acknowledge God to be the only true God and our God, and to worship and glorify him accordingly.

## Proofs
- The first commandment requireth us to know and acknowledge God to be the only true God and our God, (1 Chronicles 28:9; Deuteronomy 26:17)
- and to worship and glorify him accordingly. (Matthew 4:10; Psalm 29:2)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is it our duty to know God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Know thou the God of thy fathers, (1 Chronicles 28:9)

**Q:** And to acknowledge him?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** In all thy ways acknowledge him, (Proverbs 3:6)

**Q:** Should we do this by an humble fear of him?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I fear God, (Genesis 42:18)

**Q:** And by an hearty confidence in him?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I trust in thee, (Psalm 25:2)

**Q:** And by an entire submission to him?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Submit yourselves unto God, (James 4:7)

### Practical response

**Q:** Should we acknowledge God as our God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** One shall say I am the Lord’s, (Isaiah 44:5)

**Q:** And as the only true God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ye turned from idols to serve the living and true God, (1 Thessalonians 1:9)

**Q:** Should we do this publicly and in the face of the world?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** And hast professed a good profession before many witnesses, (1 Timothy 6:12)

**Q:** Should we do it with judgment and understanding?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Choose ye this day whom ye will serve, (Joshua 24:15)

**Q:** Should ye do it joyfully and without regret?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The jailer rejoiced, believing in God with all his house, (Acts 16:34)

**Q:** Should we do it sincerely and without deceit?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Son give me thine heart, (Proverbs 23:26)

**Q:** Should we do it entirely and without reserve?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Caleb hath wholly followed the Lord, (Deuteronomy 1:36)

**Q:** Should we do it resolutely and without hesitation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Nay, but we will serve the Lord, (Joshua 24:21)

**Q:** And should we do it fixedly and without revolt?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** So will we not go back from thee, (Psalm 80:18)

### Practical response

**Q:** Should we give up ourselves to the will of God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Here am I, let him do to me as seemeth him good, (2 Samuel 15:26)

**Q:** And surrender ourselves to his government?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** By thee only will we make mention of thy name, (Isaiah 26:13)

**Q:** And devote ourselves to his service?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Yield yourselves unto the Lord, and serve the Lord your God, (2 Chronicles 30:8)

**Q:** And has God a just right to all this?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Of thine own have we given unto thee, (1 Chronicles 29:14)

### Section 4

**Q:** Is it enough that we verbally devote ourselves to the Lord?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** For we must vow and pay unto the Lord our God, (Psalm 76:11)

**Q:** Must we therefore worship him?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** We are an holy priesthood to offer up spiritual sacrifices unto God, (1 Peter 2:5)

**Q:** Must we do this with the inward man?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Bless the Lord, O my soul, (Psalm 103:1)

**Q:** And with the whole man?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** All that is within me bless his holy name, (Psalm 103:1)

**Q:** And with a view to Christ?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Their sacrifices shall be accepted upon mine altar, (Isaiah 56:7)

**Q:** And by the assistance of the Spirit?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Strengthened with might by his Spirit in the inward man, (Ephesians 3:16)

**Q:** And in the way of his own appointments?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For in vain do they worship me, teaching for doctrines the commandments of men, (Matthew 15:9)

### Practical response

**Q:** Should we also glorify God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Glorify God in your body, and in your spirit, (1 Corinthians 6:20)

**Q:** Should we do this by a constant attendance on his ordinances?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** All nations shall come and worship before thee, and shall glorify thy name, (Psalm 86:9)

**Q:** And by an humble confession of our sins?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** My son, give glory to God, and make confession to him, (Joshua 7:19)

**Q:** And by a regular course of obedience?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Herein is my Father glorified, if ye bring forth much fruit, (John 15:8)

**Q:** And if called thereto by suffering for his sake?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** This spake he, signifying by what death he should glorify God, (John 21:19)

**Q:** And will this in the end redound to our own advantage?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For them that honour me, I will honour, (1 Samuel 2:30)
