Death being threatened as the wages of sin, it is appointed unto all men once to die; for that all have sinned.

## Proofs
- Death being threatened as the wages of sin, (Romans 6:23)
- it is appointed unto all men once to die; (Hebrews 9:27)
- for that all have sinned. (Romans 5:12)
