title: What do you believe concerning the forgiveness of sins?
slug: hc-56
categories:
  - hc
---

I believe that God, because of Christ’s satisfaction, will no more remember my sins, nor my sinful nature, against which I have to struggle all my life, but He will graciously grant me the righteousness of Christ, that I may never come into condemnation.

## Proofs
- I believe that God, because of Christ’s satisfaction, will no more remember my sins, (Psalm 103:3–4; Psalm 103:10; Psalm 103:12; Micah 7:18–19; 2 Corinthians 5:18–21; 1 John 1:7; 1 John 2:2)
- nor my sinful nature, against which I have to struggle all my life, (Romans 7:21–25)
- but He will graciously grant me the righteousness of Christ, that I may never come into condemnation. (John 3:17–18; John 5:24; Romans 8:1–2)
