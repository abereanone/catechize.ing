Christ ascended into heaven to manifest Himself there as Head of His Church, through whom the Father governs all things.

## Proofs
- Christ ascended into heaven to manifest Himself there as Head of His Church, (Ephesians 1:20–23; Colossians 1:18)
- through whom the Father governs all things. (Matthew 28:18; John 5:22–23)
