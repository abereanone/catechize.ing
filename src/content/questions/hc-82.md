No, for then the covenant of God would be profaned and His wrath kindled against the whole congregation. Therefore, according to the command of Christ and His apostles, the Christian church is duty-bound to exclude such persons by the keys of the kingdom of heaven, until they amend their lives.

## Proofs
- No, for then the covenant of God would be profaned and His wrath kindled against the whole congregation. (Psalm 50:16; Isaiah 1:11–17; 1 Corinthians 11:17–34)
- Therefore, according to the command of Christ and His apostles, the Christian church is duty-bound to exclude such persons by the keys of the kingdom of heaven, until they amend their lives.
