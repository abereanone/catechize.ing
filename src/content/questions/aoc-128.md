Yea, for seeing both our Body and Soul are the Temples of the Holy Ghost, God will have us to possess both in Purity and Holiness, and therefore Deeds, Gestures, Words, Thoughts, filthy Lusts, and whatsoever inticeth a Man unto these, all these God wholly forbiddeth.

## Proofs
- Yea, for seeing both our Body and Soul are the Temples of the Holy Ghost, God will have us to possess both in Purity and Holiness, and therefore Deeds, (Ephesians 5:31; 1 Corinthians 6:18,20)
- Gestures, Words, (Matthew 5:27–28)
- Thoughts, filthy Lusts, and whatsoever inticeth a Man unto these, all these God (Ephesians 5:18; Job 31:1; Psalm 39:1)
- wholly forbiddeth.
