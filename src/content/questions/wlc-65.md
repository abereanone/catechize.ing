title: What special benefits do the members of the invisible church enjoy by Christ?
slug: wlc-65
categories:
  - wlc

The members of the invisible church by Christ enjoy union and communion with him in grace and glory.

## Proofs
- The members of the invisible church by Christ enjoy union and communion with him in grace and glory. (John 17:21; Ephesians 2:5–6; John 17:24)
