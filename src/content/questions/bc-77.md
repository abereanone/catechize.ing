## Proofs
- The seventh commandment forbiddeth all unchaste thoughts, words, and actions. (Matthew 15:19; 5:28; Ephesians 5:3–4)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is adultery a very great sin?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** It is called an abomination, (Ezekiel 22:11)

**Q:** Doth it waste the body?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** And thou mourn at last when thy flesh and thy body are consumed, (Proverbs 5:11)

**Q:** And wrong the soul?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Abstain from fleshly lusts, that war against the soul, (1 Peter 2:11)

**Q:** And bring a blot upon the character?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** A wound and dishonour shall they get, (Proverbs 6:33)

**Q:** Is it a besotting sin?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** It takes away the heart, (Hosea 4:11)

**Q:** And an impoverishing sin?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** By means of a whorish woman a man is brought to a piece of bread, (Proverbs 6:26)

**Q:** Is it a kind of sacrilege?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Shall I take the members of Christ and make them the members of a harlot, (1 Corinthians 6:15)

**Q:** Doth it unfit for the fellowship of the saints?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I have written unto you not to keep company if any man that is called a brother be a fornicator, (1 Corinthians 5:11)

**Q:** And will it be severely punished both in this world and in that which is to come?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For whoremongers and adulterers God will judge, (Hebrews 13:4)

**Q:** 5 Is fornication as well as adultery a sin?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Mortify your members which are upon earth, fornication, (Colossians 3:5)

**Q:** And incest?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** None of you shall approach to any that is near of kin to him, (Leviticus 18:6)

**Q:** And polygamy?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Did not he make one? yet he had the residue of the spirit, (Malachi 2:15)

**Q:** And all manner of uncleanness?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** But fornication and uncleanness, let it not be once named amongst you, (Ephesians 5:3)

### Section 2

**Q:** Are unclean desires sinful?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** We must mortify inordinate affection and evil concupiscence, (Colossians 3:5)

**Q:** And unclean reflections?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** She multiplied her whoredoms by calling to remembrance the days of her youth, (Ezekiel 23:19)

**Q:** And unchaste looks?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Having eyes full of adultery, (2 Peter 2:14)

**Q:** And immodest apparel?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** There met him a woman with the attire of a harlot, (Proverbs 7:10)

**Q:** And obscene words?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Neither filthiness nor foolish talking, (Ephesians 5:4)

**Q:** And lascivious actions?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Such as chambering and wantonness, (Romans 13:13)

**Q:** And that intemperance and idleness which lead hereto’?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** This was the iniquity of Sodom, pride, idleness, and fulness of bread, and she committed abomination before me, (Ezekiel 16:49–50)
