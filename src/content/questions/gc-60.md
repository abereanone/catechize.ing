title: "Is there greater importance in His having been crucified than if He had been put death in another way?"
slug: gc-60
categories:
  - gc
---

Yes, as Paul also shows us when he says that He hanged on a tree to take our curse upon Himself and acquit us of it (Gal 3:13). For that kind of death was accursed of God (Deu 21:23).
