Because He has ransomed us, body and soul, from all our sins, not with silver or gold but with His precious blood, and has freed us from all the power of the devil to make us His own possession.

## Proofs
- Because He has ransomed us, body and soul, (1 Corinthians 6:20; 1 Timothy 2:5–6)
- from all our sins, not with silver or gold but with His precious blood, (1 Peter 1:18–19)
- and has freed us from all the power of the devil to make us His own possession. (Colossians 1:13–14; Hebrews 2:14–15)
