Christ executeth the office of a prophet, in revealing to us, by his Word and Spirit the will of God for our salvation.

## Proofs
- Christ executeth the office of a prophet, in revealing to us, by his Word and Spirit the will of God for our salvation. (John 1:18; 1 Peter 1:10–12; John 15:15; John 20:31)
