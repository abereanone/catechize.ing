title: Do those believe in the only Saviour Jesus who seek their salvation and well-being from saints, in themselves, or anywhere else?
slug: hc-30

No. Though they boast of Him in words, they in fact deny the only Saviour Jesus. For one of two things must be true: either Jesus is not a complete Saviour, or those who by true faith accept this Saviour must find in Him all that is necessary for their salvation.

## Proofs
- No. Though they boast of Him in words, they in fact deny the only Saviour Jesus. (1 Corinthians 1:12–13; Galatians 5:4)
- For one of two things must be true: either Jesus is not a complete Saviour, or those who by true faith accept this Saviour must find in Him all that is necessary for their salvation. (Colossians 1:19–20; Colossians 2:10; 1 John 1:7)
