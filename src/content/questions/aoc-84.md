It is not only to imbrace, by an assured Confidence of Mind, the whole Passion and Death of Christ, and thereby, to obtain Forgiveness of Sins, and everlasting Life, but also by the Holy Ghost, who dwelleth both in Christ, and us, so more and more to be united to his sacred Body, that though he be in Heaven, and we on Earth, yet nevertheless we are Flesh of his Flesh, and Bone of his Bones: and as all the Members of the Body are quickned by one Soul: so are we also quickned and guided by one and the same Spirit.

## Proofs
- It is not only to imbrace, by an assured Confidence of Mind, the whole Passion and Death of Christ, and thereby, (John 6:35,40,47–48,50–51,53–54)
- to obtain Forgiveness of Sins, and everlasting Life, but also by the Holy Ghost, who dwelleth both in Christ, and us, so more and more to (John 6:56)
- be united to his sacred Body, that though he be (Acts 3:21; Acts 1:9,&c; 1 Corinthians 11:26)
- in Heaven, and we on Earth, yet nevertheless we are Flesh of his Flesh, and Bone of his (Ephesians 5:29–30,32; 1 Corinthians 6:15,17,19; 1 John 3:24; 1 John 4:13; John 14:23)
- Bones: and as all the Members of the Body are quickned by one Soul: so are we also quickned and (John 6:56–58; John 15:1–6; Ephesians 4:15–16)
- guided by one and the same Spirit.
