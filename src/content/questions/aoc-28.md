Because he saveth us from our sins; neither ought any safety to be sought for from any other, nor can elsewhere be found.

## Proofs
- Because he saveth us from our (Matthew 1:21)
- sins; neither ought any safety to be sought for from any other, (Hebrews 7:25; Acts 4:12)
- nor can elsewhere be found.
