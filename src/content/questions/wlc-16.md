God created all the angels spirits, immortal, holy, excelling in knowledge, mighty in power, to execute his commandments, and to praise his name, yet subject to change.

## Proofs
- God created all the angels (Colossians 1:16)
- spirits, (Psalm 104:4)
- immortal, (Matthew 22:30)
- holy, (Matthew 25:31)
- excelling in knowledge, (2 Samuel 14:17; Matthew 24:36)
- mighty in power, (2 Thessalonians 1:7)
- to execute his commandments, and to praise his name, (Psalm 103:20–21)
- yet subject to change. (2 Peter 2:4)
