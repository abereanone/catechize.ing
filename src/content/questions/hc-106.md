By forbidding murder God teaches us that He hates the root of murder, such as envy, hatred, anger, and desire of revenge, and that He regards all these as murder.

## Proofs
- By forbidding murder God teaches us that He hates the root of murder, such as envy, hatred, anger, and desire of revenge, (Proverbs 14:30; Romans 1:29; Romans 12:19; Galatians 5:19–21; James 1:20; 1 John 2:9–11)
- and that He regards all these as murder. (1 John 3:15)
