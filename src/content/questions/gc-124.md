title: "But can we say from this that a Christian man is justified by works after God has called him, or that through them he merits the love of God, and so obtains eternal life?"
slug: gc-124
categories:
  - gc
---

No. On the contrary, it is said that no man living will be justified in His sight (Ps 143:2). Therefore we have to pray that He will not enter into judgment with us, nor call us to account.
