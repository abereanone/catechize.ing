The preface to the ten commandments teacheth us that because God is the Lord, and our God and redeemer, therefore we are bound to keep all his commandments.

## Proofs
- The preface to the ten commandments teacheth us that because God is the Lord, and our God and redeemer, therefore we are bound to keep all his commandments. (Luke 1:74–75; 1 Peter 1:15–19)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is God the Lord?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I am the Lord, that is my name, (Isaiah 42:8)

**Q:** Is this a name betokening authority?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The Lord Most High is terrible, he is a great King, (Psalm 47:2)

**Q:** Is it therefore an obligation upon us to keep his commands?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ye shall therefore keep my statutes and my judgments, I am the Lord, (Leviticus 18:5)

**Q:** Is he our God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** O Lord, thou art our God, (2 Chronicles 14:11)

**Q:** Is he so by covenant?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I entered into covenant with thee, and thou becamest mine, (Ezekiel 16:8)

**Q:** And by our choice?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ye have chosen you the Lord, (Joshua 24:22)

**Q:** Is this a reason why we should keep his commandments?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou shalt love the Lord thy God, and keep his charge, (Deuteronomy 11:1)

**Q:** And is it a reason obligatory to Gentiles as well as Jews?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For is he the God of the Jews only? is he not also of the Gentiles? Yea of the Gentiles also, (Romans 3:29)

### Section 2

**Q:** Is God our Redeemer?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** All flesh shall know that I the Lord am thy Saviour and thy Redeemer, (Isaiah 49:26)

**Q:** Hath he redeemed us from outward troubles?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The angel that redeemed me from all evil, bless the lads, (Genesis 48:16)

**Q:** And from our spiritual enemies?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** We are delivered out of the hands of our enemies, (Luke 1:74)

**Q:** And from the bondage of sin?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He shall redeem Israel from all their iniquities, (Psalm 130:8)

**Q:** And from eternal wrath?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Who hath delivered us from the wrath to come, (1 Thessalonians 1:10)

### Section 3

**Q:** Are we then in gratitude bound to obey God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Truly I am thy servant, I am thy servant, thou hast loosed my bonds, (Psalm 116:16)

**Q:** And in justice?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For ye are not your own, (1 Corinthians 6:19)

**Q:** And are we guilty not only of horrid ingratitude, but of great impiety if we do not?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For will a man rob God? (Malachi 3:8)
