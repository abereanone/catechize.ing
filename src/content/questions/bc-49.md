title: What doth the preface to the ten commandments teach us?
slug: bc-49
categories:
  - bc
---

The preface to the ten commandments teacheth us that because God is the Lord, and our God and redeemer, therefore we are bound to keep all his commandments.

## Proofs
- The preface to the ten commandments teacheth us that because God is the Lord, and our God and redeemer, therefore we are bound to keep all his commandments. (Luke 1:74–75; 1 Peter 1:15–19)
