id: 203
title: "What more then does it mean?"
slug: gc-203
categories:
  - gc
---

Since our bodies and our souls are temples of the Holy Spirit (1Co 3:16, 6:15; 2Co 6:16), we must preserve them in uprightness. And so we must be chaste not only in deed, but also in desire, word and gesture. Accordingly no part of us is to be polluted with unchastity.
