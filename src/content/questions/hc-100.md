Certainly, for no sin is greater or provokes God’s wrath more than the blaspheming of His Name. That is why He commanded it to be punished with death.

## Proofs
- Certainly, (Leviticus 5:1)
- for no sin is greater or provokes God’s wrath more than the blaspheming of His Name. That is why He commanded it to be punished with death. (Leviticus 24:16)
