From the Holy Spirit, who works it in our hearts by the preaching of the gospel, and strengthens it by the use of the sacraments.

## Proofs
- From the Holy Spirit, (John 3:5; 1 Corinthians 2:10–14; Ephesians 2:8; Philippians 1:29)
- who works it in our hearts by the preaching of the gospel, (Romans 10:17; 1 Peter 1:23–25)
- and strengthens it by the use of the sacraments. (Matthew 28:19–20; 1 Corinthians 10:16)
