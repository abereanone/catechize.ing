## Proofs
- The eighth commandment is, Thou shalt not steal. (Exodus 20:15)

## Additional Exposition

<a href="https://baptistcatechism.org/beddome/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">From Benjamin Beddome</a>


### Section 1

**Q:** Hath God a supreme and sovereign right over all things?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He is Lord of all, (Acts 10:36)

**Q:** But have men a delegated right over their lawful possessions?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The earth, God hath given to the children of men, (Psalm 115:16)

**Q:** Is it a sin therefore to deprive any of this their right?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ye shall not steal nor deal falsely, (Leviticus 19:11)

**Q:** And was such a prohibition necessary?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For every brother will utterly supplant, (Jeremiah 9:4)
