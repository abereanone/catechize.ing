That he might by the Power of his Godhead sustain in his flesh the burthen of God’s wrath, and might recover and restore unto us that Righteousness and Life which we lost.

## Proofs
- That he might by the Power of his (Isaiah 55:3,8; Acts 2:24; 1 Peter 3:18)
- Godhead sustain in his flesh the burthen of God’s wrath, (1 John 1:2; 1 John 4:9–10; Acts 20:28; John 3:16)
- and might recover and restore unto us that Righteousness and Life which we lost.
