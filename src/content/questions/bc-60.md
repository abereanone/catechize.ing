The third commandment forbiddeth all profaning and abusing of any thing whereby God makes himself known.

## Proofs
- The third commandment forbiddeth all profaning and abusing of any thing whereby God makes himself known. (Malachi 1:6–7, 12; 2:2; 3:14)
