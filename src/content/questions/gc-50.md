title: "What do you mean by the two clauses, \"Conceived of the Holy Ghost, born of the Virgin Mary\""
slug: gc-50
categories:
  - gc
---

That He was formed in womb of the Virgin Mary, of her proper substance, to be the seed of David, as had been foretold (Ps 132:11), and yet that this was wrought by the miraculous operation of the Holy Spirit, without the cooperation of a man (Mat 1:18; Luke 1:35).
