That I am righteous in Christ before God, and an Heir of eternal Life.

## Proofs
- That I am righteous in Christ before God, and an Heir (Romans 1:17; John 3:36; Romans 3:4,22,24–25,28; Romans 5:1; Galatians 2:16; Ephesians 2:8–9)
- of eternal Life.
