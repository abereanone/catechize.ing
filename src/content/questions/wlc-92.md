title: What did God at first reveal unto man as the rule of his obedience?
slug: wlc-92
categories:
  - wlc
---

The rule of obedience revealed to Adam in the estate of innocence, and to all mankind in him, besides a special command not to eat of the fruit of the tree of the knowledge of good and evil, was the moral law.

## Proofs
- The rule of obedience revealed to Adam in the estate of innocence, and to all mankind in him, besides a special command not to eat of the fruit of the tree of the knowledge of good and evil, was the moral law. (Genesis 1:26–27; Romans 2:14–15; 10:5; Genesis 2:17)
