The covenant of grace was made with Christ as the second Adam, and in him with all the elect as his seed.

## Proofs
- The covenant of grace was made with Christ as the second Adam, and in him with all the elect as his seed. (Galatians 3:16; Romans 5:15–21; Isaiah 53:10–11)
