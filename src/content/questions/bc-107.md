The preface of the Lord’s prayer, which is Our Father which art in heaven, teacheth us to draw near to God with all holy reverence and confidence, as children to a father, able and ready to help us; and that we should pray with and for others.

## Proofs
- The preface of the Lord’s prayer, which is Our Father which art in heaven, (Matthew 6:9)
- teacheth us to draw near to God with all holy reverence and confidence, as children to a father, able and ready to help us; (Romans 8:15; Luke 11:13; Psalm 24:8)
- and that we should pray with and for others. (Acts 12:5; 1 Timothy 2:1–2)

## Additional Exposition

<a href="https://baptistcatechism.org/beddome/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">From Benjamin Beddome</a>


### Section 1

**Q:** Is God a Father?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** O Lord, thou art our Father, (Isaiah 64:8)

**Q:** Is he the Father of all the saints?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** There is one God and Father of all, (Ephesians 4:6)

**Q:** Should they then plead this relation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Wilt thou not from this time cry unto me, my Father? (Jeremiah 3:4)

**Q:** And continually acknowledge his fatherly kindness and care?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I was cast upon thee from the womb, thou art my God from my mother’s belly, (Psalm 22:10)

**Q:** Doth this title peculiarly belonging to the first person of the ever blessed Trinity?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I will pray the Father, (John 14:16)

**Q:** But is it confined to him?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** For Christ is called the everlasting Father, (Isaiah 9:6)

### Practical response

**Q:** Should we take occasion from hence to approach God with reverence?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** If ye call on the Father, pass the time of your sojourning here in fear, (1 Peter 1:17)

**Q:** And with delight?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I will go unto God, my exceeding joy, (Psalm 43:4)

**Q:** And with boldness?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Doubtless thou art our Father, though Abraham be ignorant of us, and Israel acknowledge us not, (Isaiah 63:16)

**Q:** And with frequency?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Evening, and morning, and at noon will I pray, (Psalm 55:17)

**Q:** And is such a temper peculiarly suited to the gospel dispensation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For ye have not received the spirit of bondage again unto fear, but the spirit of adoption, whereby ye cry, Abba, Father, (Romans 8:15)

### Section 3

**Q:** Is God our Father in heaven?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** O Lord God of our fathers, art not thou God in heaven? (2 Chronicles 20:6)

**Q:** Doth this set forth the greatness of God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Is not God in the height of heaven? and behold the height of the stars, how high they are, (Job 22:12)

**Q:** And his omniscience?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The Lord’s throne is in heaven, his eyes behold, his eyelids try the children of men, (Psalm 11:4)

**Q:** And his power?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** God is in the heavens, he hath done whatsoever he pleased, (Psalm 115:3)

**Q:** And his holiness?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thus saith he whose name is holy, I dwell in the high and holy place, (Isaiah 57:15)

**Q:** And his absolute dominion?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the Lord hath prepared his throne in the heavens, (Psalm 103:19)

**Q:** Doth this title distinguish the great God from dumb idols?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the Most High dwelleth not in temples made with hands, (Acts 7:48)

**Q:** And from frail men?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For we dwell in houses of clay, and our foundation is in the dust, (Job 4:19)

### Section 4

**Q:** Is this a ground for humility in prayer?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** God is in heaven and thou upon earth, therefore let thy words be few, (Ecclesiastes 5:2)

**Q:** And for confidence?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Hear thou in heaven, and do, (1 Kings 8:32)

**Q:** And for heavenly-mindedness?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Let us lift up our hearts with our hands unto God in the heavens, (Lamentations 3:41)

### Section 5

**Q:** Must we pray with others?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** There were many gathered together, praying, (Acts 12:12)

**Q:** And for others?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** We must make supplication for all saints, (Ephesians 6:11)

**Q:** And shall prayers put up on earth find acceptance in heaven?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I will cry unto God most high, he shall send from heaven and save me, (Psalm 57:2–3)
