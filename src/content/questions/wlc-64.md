The invisible church is the whole number of the elect, that have been, are, or shall be gathered into one under Christ the head.

## Proofs
- The invisible church is the whole number of the elect, that have been, are, or shall be gathered into one under Christ the head. (Ephesians 1:10, 22–23; John 10:16; 11:52)
