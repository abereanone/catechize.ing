That the word may become effectual to salvation, we must attend thereunto with diligence, preparation, and prayer; receive it with faith and love, lay it up in our hearts, and practice it in our lives.

## Proofs
- That the word may become effectual to salvation, we must attend thereunto with diligence, (Proverbs 8:34)
- preparation, (1 Peter 2:1–2)
- and prayer; (Psalm 119:18)
- receive it with faith and love, (Hebrews 4:2; 2 Thessalonians 2:10)
- lay it up in our hearts, (Psalm 119:18)
- and practice it in our lives. (Luke 8:15; James 1:25)

## Additional Exposition

<a href="https://baptistcatechism.org/beddome/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">From Benjamin Beddome</a>


### Section 1

**Q:** That the word of God may profit, should we attend thereto with diligence?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** All the people were attentive to hear him, (Luke 19:48)

**Q:** And be inquisitive into the meaning of it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Of whom speaketh the prophet this? of himself, or of some other man? (Acts 8:34)

**Q:** And compare one scripture with another?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Comparing spiritual things with spiritual, (1 Corinthians 2:13)

### Section 2

**Q:** Is preparation also necessary in order to our profiting by the word?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ezra prepared his heart to seek the law of the Lord, (Ezra 7:10)

**Q:** Should we for this purpose lay aside worldly cares?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the thorns choke the seed, (Matthew 13:7)

**Q:** And carnal passions?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** All malice, and guile, and hypocrisies, and envies, and evil speakings, (1 Peter 2:1)

**Q:** And all pre-imbibed prejudices?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Not like those who cried what will the babbler say? (Acts 17:18)

**Q:** Should we pray for our ministers?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** And for me that utterance may be given unto me, (Ephesians 6:19)

**Q:** And for ourselves?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Teach me thy judgments, (Psalm 119:108)

**Q:** Should we also come with desire?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** As new born babes desire the sincere milk of the word, (1 Peter 2:2)

**Q:** And with expectation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** My soul, wait thou only on God, for my expectation is from him, (Psalm 62:5)

**Q:** And will such a preparation of the heart be greatly to our advantage?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou wilt prepare their heart, thou wilt cause thine ear to hear, (Psalm 10:17)

**Q:** But will a want of preparation excuse a neglect of duty?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** For a multitude of the people had not cleansed themselves, yet did they eat of the passover, (2 Chronicles 30:18)

### Practical response

**Q:** Should the word be received in faith?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thus we read of the hearing of faith, (Galatians 3:2)

**Q:** And in love?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Consider how I love thy precepts, (Psalm 119:159)

**Q:** And will a want of this be a hindrance to our profiting?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They received not the love of the truth, that they might be saved? (2 Thessalonians 2:10)

### Practical response

**Q:** Should we hear the word with reverence?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** When Ezra opened the book all the people stood up, (Nehemiah 8:5)

**Q:** And with judgment?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the ear trieth words as the mouth tasteth meat, (Job 34:3)

**Q:** And with care?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Take heed how ye hear, (Luke 8:18)

**Q:** Must we keep from roving thoughts in hearing?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Their heart goeth after their covetousness, (Ezekiel 33:31)

**Q:** And from a wandering eye?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The eyes of all were fastened on him, (Luke 4:20)

**Q:** And from sleep and drowsiness?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Could not ye watch with me one hour? (Matthew 26:40)

### Practical response

**Q:** Should we treasure up what we hear?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Remember how ye have heard and received, and hold fast, (Revelation 3:3)

**Q:** Should we therefore meditate upon it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** And she pondered all these things in her heart, (Luke 2:19)

**Q:** And talk of it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** And shalt talk of them when thou sittest in thine house, and when thou walkest in the way, (Deuteronomy 6:7)

**Q:** And is this necessary in order to our profiting by it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** By which also ye are saved, if ye keep in memory what I preached unto you, (1 Corinthians 15:2)

### Section 6

**Q:** Is it also requisite that we practice what we hear?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** As thou hast said, so must we do, (Ezra 10:12)

**Q:** And is this the way to obtain the blessing?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** This man shall be blessed in his deed, (James 1:25)

**Q:** And to avoid the curse?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For some will say, thou hast taught in our streets; but he will reply, I know not whence you are, (Luke 13:26)
