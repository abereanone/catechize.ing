That the word may become effectual to salvation, we must attend thereunto with diligence, preparation, and prayer; receive it with faith and love, lay it up in our hearts, and practice it in our lives.

## Proofs
- That the word may become effectual to salvation, we must attend thereunto with diligence, (Proverbs 8:34)
- preparation, (1 Peter 2:1–2)
- and prayer; (Psalm 119:18)
- receive it with faith and love, (Hebrews 4:2; 2 Thessalonians 2:10)
- lay it up in our hearts, (Psalm 119:18)
- and practice it in our lives. (Luke 8:15; James 1:25)
