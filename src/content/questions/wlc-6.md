The scriptures make known what God is, the persons in the Godhead, his decrees, and the execution of his decrees.

## Proofs
- The scriptures make known what God is, (Hebrews 11:6)
- the persons in the Godhead, (Matthew 3:16–17)
- his decrees, (Acts 15:14–15, 17–18; Isaiah 46:9)
- and the execution of his decrees. (Acts 4:27–28)
