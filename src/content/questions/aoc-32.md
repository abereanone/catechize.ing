title: For what cause is Christ called the only begotten Son of God, when we also are the Sons of God?
slug: aoc-32
categories:
  - aoc
---

Because Christ alone is the eternal and natural Son of the eternal Father, and we are but Sons adopted of the Father by Grace for his sake.

## Proofs
- Because Christ alone is the eternal and natural Son of the (Hebrews 1:2–3; John 3:16)
- eternal Father, and (Romans 8:3; Ephesians 1:6; John 1:12; 1 John 1:3)
- we are but Sons adopted of the Father by Grace for his sake.
