title: "How is that He died to suffer the punishment due to us, and thus to deliver us from it?"
slug: gc-57
categories:
  - gc
---

However, because we were guilty before the judgment of God as evil-doers, in order to represent us in person He was pleased to appear before the tribunal of an earthly judge, and to be condemned by his mouth, that we might be acquitted before the throne of the celestial Judge.
