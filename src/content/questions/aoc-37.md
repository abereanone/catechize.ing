title: For what cause should he suffer under Pilate, as being his Judg?
slug: aoc-37
categories:
  - aoc
---

That he being innocent and condemned before a civil Judg might deliver us from the severe Judgment of God which remained for all men.

## Proofs
- That he being innocent (Luke 23:14; John 19:4)
- and condemned before a civil Judg (Psalm 69:4; Isaiah 53:4–5; 2 Corinthians 5:21; Galatians 3:13)
- might deliver us from the severe Judgment of God which remained for all men.
