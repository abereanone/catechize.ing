The word of God is to be preached only by such as are sufficiently gifted, and also duly approved and called to that office.

## Proofs
- The word of God is to be preached only by such as are sufficiently gifted, (1 Timothy 3:2, 6; Ephesians 4:8–11; Hosea 4:6; Malachi 2:7; 2 Corinthians 3:6)
- and also duly approved and called to that office. (Jeremiah 14:15; Romans 10:15; Hebrews 5:4; 1 Corinthians 12:28–29; 1 Timothy 3:10; 4:14; 5:22)
