id: 42
title: Since Christ has died for us, why do we still have to die?
slug: hc-42
categories:
  - hc
---

Our death is not a payment for our sins, but it puts an end to sin and is an entrance into eternal life.

## Proofs
- Our death is not a payment for our sins, but it puts an end to sin and is an entrance into eternal life. (John 5:24; Philippians 1:21–23; 1 Thessalonians 5:9–10)
