It is not enough; for when God condemneth Anger, Envy, Hatred, he requireth that we love our Neighbour as our selves and that we use Humanity, Lenity, Courtesie, Patience, and Mercy towards him, and turn away from him as much as we may whatsoever may be hurtful unto him; in a word, that we be so affected in Mind as that we stick not to do good also unto our Enemies.

## Proofs
- It is not enough; for when God condemneth Anger, Envy, Hatred, he requireth that (Matthew 22:39; Matthew 7:12)
- we love our Neighbour as our selves and that we (Romans 12:10)
- use Humanity, Lenity, (Ephesians 4:2; Galatians 6:1–2; Matthew 5:5; Romans 12:18; Matthew 5:7; Luke 6:36)
- Courtesie, Patience, (Exodus 23:5)
- and Mercy towards him, and (Matthew 5:45)
- turn away from him as much as we may whatsoever may be hurtful unto him; in a word, that we be so affected in Mind as that we (Romans 12:20)
- stick not to do good also unto our Enemies.
