title: Canons of Dort, 1st Head, Article I - Of Divine Predestination
slug: cd-1
categories:
  - cd
---

As all men have sinned in Adam, lie under the curse, and are obnoxious to eternal death, God would have done no injustice by leaving them all to perish, and delivering them over to condemnation on account of sin, according to the words of the apostle. Romans 3:19, that every mouth may be stopped, and all the world may become guilty before God. And Romans 6:23, for the wages of sin is death.

*Source: Public-domain 1840 translation from Wikisource (The Constitution of the Reformed Dutch Church of North America, "Canons of the Synod of Dordrecht").*
