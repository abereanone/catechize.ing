title: But may images not be tolerated in the churches as “books for the laity”?
slug: hc-98
categories:

---

No, for we should not be wiser than God. He wants His people to be taught not by means of dumb images but by the living preaching of His Word.

## Proofs
- No, for we should not be wiser than God. He wants His people to be taught not by means of dumb images (Jeremiah 10:8; Habakkuk 2:18–20)
- but by the living preaching of His Word. (Romans 10:14–15; Romans 10:17; 2 Timothy 3:16–17; 2 Peter 1:19)
