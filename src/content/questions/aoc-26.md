The almighty Power of God, every where present, whereby he doth as it were by his hand uphold and govern Heaven and Earth, with all the Creatures therein, so that those things which grow in the Earth, as likewise rain and drowth, fruitfulness and barenness, meat and and drink, health and sickness, riches and poverty; in a word, all things come not rashly and by chance, but by his fatherly Counsel and will.

## Proofs
- The almighty Power of God, every where (Acts 17:25; Psalm 94:9; Isaiah 29:15; Ezekiel 8:12)
- present, whereby he doth as it were by his hand uphold (Hebrews 1:2–3)
- and govern Heaven and Earth, with all the Creatures therein, so that those things which grow in the Earth, as likewise rain and drowth, fruitfulness and barenness, meat (Jeremiah 5:24; Acts 14:17)
- and (John 9:3)
- and drink, health and (Proverbs 22:2)
- sickness, riches and poverty; in a word, all things come not rashly and by chance, but by his fatherly Counsel and will.
