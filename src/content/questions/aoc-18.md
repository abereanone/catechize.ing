## Proofs
- Even our Lord Jesus (Matthew 1:23; 1 Timothy 3:16; John 14:16; 1 Timothy 2:5; Luke 2:11)
- Christ, who is made to us of God’s Wisdom, Righteousness, Sanctification (1 Corinthians 1:30)
- & Redemption.
