Because of the justice and truth of God satisfaction for our sins could be made in no other way than by the death of the Son of God.

## Proofs
- Because of the justice and truth of God (Genesis 2:17)
- satisfaction for our sins could be made in no other way than by the death of the Son of God. (Romans 8:3; Philippians 2:8; Hebrews 2:9; Hebrews 2:14–15)
