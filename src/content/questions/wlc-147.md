The duties required in the tenth commandment are, such a full contentment with our own condition, and such a charitable frame of the whole soul toward our neighbor, as that all our inward motions and affections touching him, tend unto, and further all that good which is his.

## Proofs
- The duties required in the tenth commandment are, such a full contentment with our own condition, (Hebrews 13:5; 1 Timothy 6:6)
- and such a charitable frame of the whole soul toward our neighbor, as that all our inward motions and affections touching him, tend unto, and further all that good which is his. (Job 31:29; Romans 12:15; Psalm 122:7–9; Esther 10:3; 1 Corinthians 13:4–7)
