The ninth commandment forbiddeth whatsoever is prejudicial to the truth, or injurious to our own or our neighbour’s good name.

## Proofs
- The ninth commandment forbiddeth whatsoever is prejudicial to the truth, or injurious to our own or our neighbour’s good name. (1 Samuel 17:28; Leviticus 19:16; Psalm 15:2–3)
