In the first petition, which is, Hallowed be thy name, we pray that God would enable us and others to glorify him in all that whereby he maketh himself known, and that he would dispose all things to his own glory.

## Proofs
- In the first petition, which is, Hallowed be thy name, (Matthew 6:9)
- we pray that God would enable us and others to glorify him in all that whereby he maketh himself known, (Psalm 67:2–3)
- and that he would dispose all things to his own glory. (Psalm 83 throughout; Romans 11:36)
