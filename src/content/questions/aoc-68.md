Immersion or dipping of the Person in Water in the Name of the Father, Son, and Holy Ghost, by such who are duly qualified by Christ.

## Proofs
- Immersion or dipping of the Person in Water in the Name of the Father, Son, and Holy Ghost, by such who are duly qualified by Christ. (Matthew 3:16; John 3:23; Acts 8:38–39; Romans 6:4)
