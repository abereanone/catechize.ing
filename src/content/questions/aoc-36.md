That he all the time of his life which he lead in the Earth, but especially at the end thereof, sustained the wrath of God, both in body and soul, against the sin of mankind, that he might by his passion, as the only propitiatory sacrifice, deliver our body and soul from everlasting damnation, and purchase us the favour of God, righteousness, and everlasting life.

## Proofs
- That he all the time of his life which he lead in the Earth, but especially at the end (1 Peter 2:4; 1 Peter 3:18; Isaiah 53:12)
- thereof, sustained the wrath of God, both in body and soul, against the sin of mankind, that he might by his passion, as the (1 John 2:2; 1 John 4:10)
- only propitiatory sacrifice, deliver our body and soul from everlasting damnation, and purchase us the favour of God, righteousness, and everlasting life.
