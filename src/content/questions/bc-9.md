There are three persons in the godhead, the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit; and these three are one God, the same in essence, equal in power and glory.

## Proofs
- There are three persons in the godhead, the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit; and these three are one God, the same in essence, equal in power and glory. (1 John 5:7; Matthew 28:19)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is there a plurality in the godhead?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For God said, Let us make man, (Genesis 1:26)

**Q:** Does the godhead consist of three persons?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** There are three that bear record in heaven, (1 John 5:7)

**Q:** Is the Father a distinct person from the Son and Spirit?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** My Father which gave them me is greater than all, (John 10:29)

**Q:** Is the Son a distinct person from the Father and Spirit?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The Lord, (i.e. the Father) said unto my Lord, (i.e. the Son) sit thou at my right hand, (Exodus 1)

**Q:** And is the Spirit a distinct person, both from the Father and the Son?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The Comforter, which is the Holy Ghost, whom the Father will send in my name, he shall teach you all things, (John 14:26)

**Q:** Are these the same in essence, affection, and operation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** These three are one, (1 John 5:7)

**Q:** May it with any propriety then be said, that there are three Gods?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** For the Lord is one, and his name is one, (Zechariah 14:9)

### Section 2

**Q:** Is the Son called God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Who is over all, God blessed for ever, (Romans 9:5)

**Q:** Is the Spirit called God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** “Why hath Satan filled thine heart to lie to the Holy Ghost, thou hast not lied unto men, but unto God, (Acts 5:3–4)

**Q:** Is the Son called Jehovah?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He is the Lord (Heb. Jehovah) our righteousness, (Jeremiah 23:6)

**Q:** Is the Spirit called Jehovah?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They tempted the Lord (Heb. Jehovah) (Exodus 17:7)

**Q:** They vexed his holy Spirit. Is this name given to any but God?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** The most high over all the earth, is he whose name alone is Jehovah, (Psalm 83:18)

### Section 3

**Q:** Is the Son eternal as well as the Father?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Before Abraham was, I am, (John 8:58)

**Q:** Is the Spirit eternal?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He is called the eternal Spirit, (Hebrews 9:14)

**Q:** Is the Son omnipresent?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Where two or three are gathered together in my name there am I, (Matthew 18:20)

**Q:** Is the Spirit so too?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Whither shall I go from thy Spirit? (Psalm 139:7)

**Q:** Is the Son omniscient?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou knowest all things, (John 21:18)

**Q:** And is the Spirit so?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He searcheth all things, (1 Corinthians 2:10)

**Q:** Is the work of creation ascribed to the Son?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** All things were made by him, (John 1:3)

**Q:** Is it also ascribed to the Spirit?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The Spirit of God hath made me, (Job 33:4)

**Q:** And is creation a work peculiar to God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He that built all things is God, (Hebrews 3:4)

### Section 4

**Q:** Is the Father the object of prayer?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Pray to thy Father which is in secret, (Matthew 6:6)

**Q:** Is the Son also the object of prayer?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Lord Jesus, receive my Spirit, (Acts 7:59)

**Q:** And the Spirit?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Grace and peace be unto you from the seven Spirits which are before the throne,* (Revelation 1:4)

**Q:** Are we baptized in the name of all the three persons in the Trinity?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost, (Matthew 28:19)

**Q:** And is religious worship a prerogative of Deity?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Worship God, (Revelation 22:9)

### Section 5

**Q:** Are divine blessings derived from all three persons in the godhead?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The grace of the Lord Jesus Christ, and the love of God, and the communion of the Holy Ghost, be with you all, (2 Corinthians 13:14)

**Q:** Have each of these their distinct province in the affair of man’s salvation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Through him we both have access by one spirit unto the Father, (Ephesians 2:18)

**Q:** Is the unity in the godhead a motive to unity among the saints?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** That they all may be one; as thou Father, art in me and I in thee, that they also may be one in us, (John 17)

**Q:** Should we hold fast this doctrine?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Hold fast the form of sound words, (2 Timothy 1:13)
