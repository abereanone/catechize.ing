Out of the Gospel which God first made known in Paradise, and afterwards did spread it a broad by the Patriarchs and Prophets: shadowed it by Sacrifices and other Ceremonies of the Law; and lastly accomplished it by his only begotten Son Christ our Lord.

## Proofs
- Out of the Gospel which God first made known (Genesis 3:15)
- in Paradise, and (Genesis 22:18; Genesis 49:10–11; Romans 1:2; Hebrews 1:1; Acts 3:22; Acts 10:43)
- afterwards did spread it a broad by the Patriarchs and (John 5:46; Hebrews 10:7)
- Prophets: shadowed it by Sacrifices and other Ceremonies of the Law; and (Romans 10:4; Galatians 4:4; Galatians 3:24; Hebrews 13:8)
- lastly accomplished it by his only begotten Son Christ our Lord.
