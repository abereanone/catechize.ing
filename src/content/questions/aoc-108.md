It is in Place of that one God, or besides that one true God who hath manifested himself in his Word and Works, to make or imagin, and account any other thing wherein thou reposest thy Hope and Confidence.

## Proofs
- It is in Place of that one God, or besides that one true God who hath manifested himself in his Word and Works, to make or imagin, and account any other thing wherein thou reposest (Philippians 3:19; Ephesians 5:5; Galatians 4:8; Ephesians 2:12; 1 John 2:23; John 5:23)
- thy Hope and Confidence.
