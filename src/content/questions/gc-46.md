---
id: 46
title: "Why do you call Him the only Son of God, seeing that God calls us all?"
slug: gc-46
categories:
  - gc
---

His children We are children of God not by nature, but only by adoption and by grace, in that God wills to regard us as such (Eph. 1:5). But the Lord Jesus who was begotten of the substance of His Father, and is of one essence with Him, is rightly called the only Son of God (John 1:14; Heb. 1:2) for there is no other who is God's Son by nature.
