Into two Tables, whereof the former delivereth in four Commandments, how we ought to behave our selves towards God: the latter delivereth in six Commandments, what duties we owe unto our Neighbours.

## Proofs
- Into (Exodus 34:28; Deuteronomy 4:13; Deuteronomy 10:3–4)
- two Tables, whereof the former delivereth in four Commandments, how we ought to behave our selves towards God: the latter delivereth in six Commandments, what duties we (Matthew 22:37–39)
- owe unto our Neighbours.
