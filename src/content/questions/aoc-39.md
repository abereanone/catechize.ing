Because the Justice and Truth of God could by no other means be satisfied for our sins, but by the very death of the Son of God.

## Proofs
- Because the Justice and Truth of God could by (Genesis 2:17)
- no other means be satisfied for our sins, but by the very death of the (Hebrews 2:9,14–15; Philippians 2:8)
- Son of God.
