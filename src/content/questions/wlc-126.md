title: What is the general scope of the fifth commandment?
slug: wlc-126
categories:
  - wlc
---

The general scope of the fifth commandment is, the performance of those duties which we mutually owe in our several relations, as inferiors, superiors, or equals.

## Proofs
- The general scope of the fifth commandment is, the performance of those duties which we mutually owe in our several relations, as inferiors, superiors, or equals. (Ephesians 5:21; 1 Peter 2:17; Romans 12:10)
