God’s works of providence are his most holy, wise, and powerful preserving and governing all his creatures; ordering them, and all their actions, to his own glory.

## Proofs
- God’s works of providence are his most holy, (Psalm 145:17)
- wise, (Psalm 104:24; Isaiah 28:29)
- and powerful preserving (Hebrews 1:3)
- and governing (Psalm 103:19)
- all his creatures; ordering them, and all their actions, (Matthew 10:29–31; Genesis 45:7)
- to his own glory. (Romans 11:36; Isaiah 63:14)
