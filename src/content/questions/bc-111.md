In the fourth petition, which is, Give us this day our daily bread, we pray that of God’s free gift we may receive a competent portion of the good things of this life, and enjoy his blessing with them.

## Proofs
- In the fourth petition, which is, Give us this day our daily bread, (Matthew 6:11)
- we pray that of God’s free gift we may receive a competent portion of the good things of this life, and enjoy his blessing with them. (Proverbs 30:8; Genesis 28:20; 1 Timothy 4:4–5)
