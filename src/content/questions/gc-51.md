---
id: 51
title: "Was it then required that He should put on our very flesh?"
slug: gc-51
categories:
  - gc
---

Yes, because it was necessary that the disobedience committed by man against God should be redressed in human nature. And moreover He could not otherwise be our Mediator to reconcile us to God His Father (1Ti 2:5; Heb 4:15).
