slug: wlc-59

Redemption is certainly applied, and effectually communicated, to all those for whom Christ hath purchased it; who are in time by the Holy Ghost enabled to believe in Christ according to the gospel.

## Proofs
- Redemption is certainly applied, and effectually communicated, to all those for whom Christ hath purchased it; (Ephesians 1:13–14; John 6:37, 39; 10:15–16)
- who are in time by the Holy Ghost enabled to believe in Christ according to the gospel. (Ephesians 2:8; 2 Corinthians 4:13)
