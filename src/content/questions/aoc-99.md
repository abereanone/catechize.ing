True Joy in God through Christ, and an earnest Desire to order thy Life according to God’s Will and to do all good Works.

## Proofs
- True Joy in God (Romans 5:1; Romans 14:17; Isaiah 57:15)
- through Christ, and an earnest Desire to order thy Life according to God’s Will and to (Romans 6:10–11; Galatians 2:20)
- do all good Works.
