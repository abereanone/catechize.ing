Immediately after the resurrection shall follow the general and final judgment of angels and men; the day and hour whereof no man knows, that all may watch and pray, and be ever ready for the coming of the Lord.

## Proofs
- Immediately after the resurrection shall follow the general and final judgment of angels and men; (2 Peter 2:4, 6–7, 14–15; Matthew 25:46)
- the day and hour whereof no man knows, that all may watch and pray, and be ever ready for the coming of the Lord. (Matthew 24:36, 42, 44)
