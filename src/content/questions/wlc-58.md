We are made partakers of the benefits which Christ hath procured, by the application of them unto us, which is the work especially of God the Holy Ghost.

## Proofs
- We are made partakers of the benefits which Christ hath procured, (John 1:11–12)
- by the application of them unto us, which is the work especially of God the Holy Ghost. (Titus 3:5–6)
