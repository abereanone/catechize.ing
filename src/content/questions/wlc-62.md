The visible church is a society made up of all such as in all ages and places of the world do profess the true religion, and of their children.

## Proofs
- The visible church is a society made up of all such as in all ages and places of the world do profess the true religion, (1 Corinthians 1:2; 12:13; Romans 15:9–12; Revelation 7:9; Psalms 2:8; 22:27–31; 45:17; Matthew 28:19–20; Isaiah 59:21)
- and of their children. (1 Corinthians 7:14; Acts 2:39; Romans 11:16; Genesis 17:7)
