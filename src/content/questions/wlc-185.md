We are to pray with an awful apprehension of the majesty of God, and deep sense of our own unworthiness, necessities, and sins; with penitent, thankful, and enlarged hearts; with understanding, faith, sincerity, fervency, love, and perseverance, waiting upon him, with humble submission to his will.

## Proofs
- We are to pray with an awful apprehension of the majesty of God, (Ecclesiastes 5:1)
- and deep sense of our own unworthiness, (Genesis 18:27; 32:10)
- necessities, (Luke 15:17–19)
- and sins; (Luke 18:13–14)
- with penitent, (Psalm 51:17)
- thankful, (Philippians 4:6)
- and enlarged hearts; (1 Samuel 1:15; 2:1)
- with understanding, (1 Corinthians 14:15)
- faith, (Mark 11:24; James 1:6)
- sincerity, (Psalms 145:18; 17:1)
- fervency, (James 5:16)
- love, (1 Timothy 2:8)
- and perseverance, (Ephesians 6:18)
- waiting upon him, (Micah 7:7)
- with humble submission to his will. (Matthew 26:39)
