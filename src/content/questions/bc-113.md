In the sixth petition, which is, And lead us not into temptation but deliver us from evil, we pray that God would either keep us from being tempted to sin, or support and deliver us when we are tempted.

## Proofs
- In the sixth petition, which is, And lead us not into temptation but deliver us from evil, (Matthew 6:13)
- we pray that God would either keep us from being tempted to sin, (Matthew 26:31)
- or support and deliver us when we are tempted. (2 Corinthians 12:8)
