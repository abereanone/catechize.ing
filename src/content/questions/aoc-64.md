title: Seeing then that only Faith maketh us Partakers of Christ and his Benefits, whence doth it proceed?
slug: aoc-64
categories:
  - aoc
---

From the Holy Ghost who kindleth it in our Hearts by the preaching of the Gospel, and other Ordinances, and confirmeth it by the use of the Sacraments.

## Proofs
- From the Holy Ghost (Ephesians 2:8; Ephesians 6:23; John 3:5; Philippians 1:29)
- who kindleth it in our Hearts by the preaching of the Gospel, and other Ordinances, and (Matthew 28:19–20; 1 Peter 1:22–23)
- confirmeth it by the use of the Sacraments.
