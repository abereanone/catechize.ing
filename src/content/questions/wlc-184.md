slug: wlc-184

We are to pray for all things tending to the glory of God, the welfare of the church, our own or others good; but not for anything that is unlawful.

## Proofs
- We are to pray for all things tending to the glory of God, (Matthew 6:9)
- the welfare of the church, (Psalms 51:18; 122:6)
- our own (Matthew 7:11)
- or others good; (Psalm 125:4)
- but not for anything that is unlawful. (1 John 5:14)
