Because God hath so manifested himself in his Word, that these three distinct Persons are that one true everlasting God.

## Proofs
- Segment (Deuteronomy 6:4; Ephesians 4:6; Isaiah 44:6; Isaiah 45:5; 1 Corinthians 8:4;)
- Because God (Isaiah 61:1; Luke 4:18; Psalm 110:1; Matthew 3:16–17; Matthew 28:19; John 14:26; John 15:26; Titus 3:5–6; Ephesians 2:18; 2 Corinthians 13:14; Galatians 4:6; 1 John 5:7)
- hath so manifested himself in his Word, that these three distinct Persons are that one true everlasting God.
