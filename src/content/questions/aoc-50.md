First, that through his Holy Spirit he poureth upon us his Members heavenly Graces, then that he shieldeth and defendeth us by his Power against all our Enemies.

## Proofs
- First, that through his Holy Spirit (Ephesians 4:16)
- he poureth upon us his Members heavenly Graces, then that he shieldeth and (Psalm 2:9; Psalm 110:2; John 10:28; Ephesians 4:8)
- defendeth us by his Power against all our Enemies.
