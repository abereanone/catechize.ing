It is not only a knowledg, whereby I surely assent to all things which God hath revealed unto us in his Word, but also an hassured trust kindled in my heart by the Holy Ghost, through the Gospel, whereby I make my repose in God, being assuredly resolved that Remission of Sins, everlasting Righteousness, and life is given not to others only, but to me also, and that freely through the Mercy of God, for for the Merits of Christ alone.

## Proofs
- It is not only a knowledg, whereby I surely assent to all things which God (Hebrews 11:13; James 2:19; Galatians 2:20)
- hath revealed unto us in his Word, but also an (Romans 4:16; Romans 5:1; Romans 10:10)
- hassured trust kindled in (Romans 1:16; Romans 10:17; 1 Corinthians 1:21; Mark 16:16; Acts 16:14)
- my heart by the Holy Ghost, through the Gospel, whereby I make my repose in God, being assuredly resolved that Remission of Sins, everlasting (Matthew 16:17; John 3:5; Galatians 5:22; Philemon 1:19)
- Righteousness, and life is given not to others only, but to me also, and that freely through the Mercy of God, for (Romans 3:24–25; Acts 10:45;)
- for the Merits of Christ alone.
