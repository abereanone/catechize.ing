The very light of nature in man, and the works of God, declare plainly that there is a God; but his word and Spirit only do sufficiently and effectually reveal him unto men for their salvation.

## Proofs
- The very light of nature in man, and the works of God, declare plainly that there is a God; (Romans 1:19–20; Psalm 19:1–3; Acts 17:28)
- but his word and Spirit only do sufficiently and effectually reveal him unto men for their salvation. (1 Corinthians 2:9–10; 2 Timothy 3:15–17; Isaiah 59:21)
