The preface to the ten commandments is in these words; I am the Lord thy God which have brought thee out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage.

## Proofs
- The preface to the ten commandments is in these words; I am the Lord thy God which have brought thee out of the land of Egypt, out of the house of bondage. (Exodus 20:2)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Were the children of Israel under peculiar obligations to God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He brought them out of the land of Egypt, and out of the house of bondage, (Exodus 20:22)

**Q:** Were the children of Israel then in Egypt?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** These are the names of the children of Israel which came into Egypt, (Exodus 1:1)

**Q:** Were they there as strangers?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ye were strangers in the land of Egypt, (Exodus 22:21)

**Q:** Were they there as bond slaves?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou wast a bondman in the land of Egypt, (Deuteronomy 15:15)

**Q:** And were they long there?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** We have dwelt in Egypt a long time, (Numbers 20:15)

### Section 2

**Q:** Did God bring them out of Egypt?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I made you to go out of Egypt, (Judges 2:1)

**Q:** And that miraculously?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ye have seen all that the Lord did before your eyes in the land of Egypt, the signs and those great miracles, (Deuteronomy 29:2–3)

**Q:** And in haste?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou comest out of Egypt in haste, (Deuteronomy 16:3)

**Q:** And at the appointed time?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** In the self same day all the hosts of the Lord went out from the land of Egypt, (Exodus 12:41)

**Q:** Did this oblige them to thankfulness?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** It was a night to be much observed to the Lord, (Exodus 12:42)

**Q:** And to obedience?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** When I brought thee out of the land of Egypt, I said, obey my voice, (Jeremiah 7:22–23)
