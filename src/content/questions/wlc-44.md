Christ executeth the office of a priest, in his once offering himself a sacrifice without spot to God, to be a reconciliation for the sins of his people; and in making continual intercession for them.

## Proofs
- Christ executeth the office of a priest, in his once offering himself a sacrifice without spot to God, (Hebrews 9:14, 28)
- to be a reconciliation for the sins of his people; (Hebrews 2:17)
- and in making continual intercession for them. (Hebrews 7:25)
