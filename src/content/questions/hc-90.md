  - hc

  - aoc-99

It is a heartfelt joy in God through Christ, and a love and delight to live according to the will of God in all good works.

## Proofs
- It is a heartfelt joy in God through Christ, (Psalm 51:8; Psalm 51:12; Isaiah 57:15; Romans 5:1; Romans 14:17)
- and a love and delight to live according to the will of God in all good works. (Romans 6:10–11; Galatians 2:20)
