The first commandment is, Thou shalt have no other gods before me.

## Proofs
- The first commandment is, Thou shalt have no other gods before me. (Exodus 20:3)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is the belief and worship of a Deity one of the principles of natural religion?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For all people will walk every one in the name of their God, (Micah 4:5)

**Q:** But is it more strongly inculcated in the divine word?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Worship the Lord, (Psalm 29:2)

### Section 2

**Q:** Did the sin of having many gods prevail in the early ages of the world?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Wherefore hast thou stolen my gods? (Genesis 31:30)

**Q:** And were God’s own people soon infected with it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Up, make us gods, (Exodus 32:1)

**Q:** Is this sin absolutely forbidden in the first commandment?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For first of all the commandments is this, hear O Israel, the Lord our God is one Lord, (Mark 12:29)

**Q:** And was it severely punished in those that were guilty of it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the Lord plagued the people because they made the calf, (Exodus 32)
