The eighth commandment requireth the lawful procuring and furthering the wealth and outward estate of ourselves and others.

## Proofs
- The eighth commandment requireth the lawful procuring and furthering the wealth and outward estate of ourselves and others. (Genesis 30:30; 1 Timothy 5:8; Leviticus 25:35; Deuteronomy 22:1–5; Exodus 23:4–5; Genesis 47:14, 20)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is it lawful for us to seek our own temporal advantage?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** And now when shall I provide for my own house, (Genesis 30:30)

**Q:** Should we do this by an application to some lawful calling?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Study to be quiet and to do your own business, (1 Thessalonians 4:11)

**Q:** And by diligence in that calling?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Not slothful in business, (Romans 12:11)

**Q:** And by frugality?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He that gathereth in the summer is a wise (Proverbs 10:5)

**Q:** And by a discreet conduct?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For by understanding is a house established, (Proverbs 24:3)

**Q:** And to all this should we join fervent prayer?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I will for this be inquired of by the house of Israel, (Ezekiel 36:37)

### Section 2

**Q:** Must we be just to all that we deal with?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Render to all their due, (Romans 13:7)

**Q:** Must we not over-reach the ignorant?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** Let no man defraud or go beyond his brother, (1 Thessalonians 4:6)

**Q:** Nor oppress the indigent?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** We must not buy the poor for silver, and the needy for a pair of shoes, (Amos 8:6)

**Q:** But should we rather relieve them?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** If thy brother be waxen poor and fallen into decay, thou shalt relieve him, (Leviticus 25:35)

**Q:** And be forward so to do?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** We must be ready to distribute, willing to communicate, (1 Timothy 6:18)

### Section 3

**Q:** Must we faithfully fulfil our promises?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I took an oath of them that they should do according to this promise, (Nehemiah 5:12)

**Q:** And be true to our trusts?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** It is required in stewards that a man be found faithful, (1 Corinthians 4:2)

**Q:** And return what we have borrowed?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the wicked borroweth and payeth not again, (Psalm 37:21)

**Q:** And discharge our just debts?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Owe no man any thing, (Romans 13:8)

**Q:** And make restitution where wrong has been done?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He shall restore that which he took violently away, or the thing which he hath deceitfully gotten, (Leviticus 6:4)

**Q:** And is such honesty the best policy?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For a little that a righteous man hath is better than the riches of many wicked, (Psalm 37:16)
