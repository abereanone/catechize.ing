---
id: 12
title: Since, according to God’s righteous judgment we deserve temporal and eternal punishment, how can we escape this punishment and be again received into favour?
slug: hc-12
categories:
  - hc
---

God demands that His justice be satisfied. Therefore full payment must be made either by ourselves or by another.

## Proofs
- God demands that His justice be satisfied. (Exodus 20:5; Exodus 23:7; Romans 2:1–11)
- Therefore full payment must be made either by ourselves or by another. (Isaiah 53:11; Romans 8:3–4)
