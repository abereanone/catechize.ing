id: 87
title: Can those be saved who do not turn to God from their ungrateful and impenitent walk of life?
slug: hc-87
categories:
  - hc
---

By no means. Scripture says that no unchaste person, idolater, adulterer, thief, greedy person, drunkard, slanderer, robber, or the like shall inherit the kingdom of God.

## Proofs
- By no means. Scripture says that no unchaste person, idolater, adulterer, thief, greedy person, drunkard, slanderer, robber, or the like shall inherit the kingdom of God. (1 Corinthians 6:9–10; Galatians 5:19–21; Ephesians 5:5–6; 1 John 3:14)
