We are not to make an image of God in any way, nor to worship Him in any other manner than He has commanded in His Word.

## Proofs
- We are not to make an image of God in any way, (Deuteronomy 4:15–19; Isaiah 40:18–25; Acts 17:29; Romans 1:23)
- nor to worship Him in any other manner than He has commanded in His Word. (Leviticus 10:1–7; Deuteronomy 12:30; 1 Samuel 15:22–23; Matthew 15:9; John 4:23–24)
