It is to grieve with heartfelt sorrow that we have offended God by our sin, and more and more to hate it and flee from it.

## Proofs
- It is to grieve with heartfelt sorrow that we have offended God by our sin, and more and more to hate it and flee from it. (Psalm 51:3–4; Psalm 51:17; Joel 2:12–13; Romans 8:12–13; 2 Corinthians 7:10)
