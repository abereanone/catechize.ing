Christ is true God, and true Man, and so according to his Manhood is not now on Earth, but according to his Godhead, his Majesty, his Grace, and Spirit is at no time from us.

## Proofs
- Segment (Matthew 28:20; Matthew 26:11; John 16:18; John 17:11; Acts 3:21)
- Christ is true God, and true Man, and so according to his Manhood is not now on Earth, but according to his Godhead, his Majesty, his Grace, and (John 14:17,&c; John 16:13; Ephesians 4:8)
- Spirit is at no time from us.
