God created man, male and female, after his own image, in knowledge, righteousness, and holiness, with dominion over the creatures.

## Proofs
- God created man, male and female, after his own image, in knowledge, righteousness, and holiness, with dominion over the creatures. (Genesis 1:26–28; Colossians 3:10; Ephesians 4:24)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Were there any men before Adam?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** He was the first man, (1 Corinthians 15:45)

**Q:** Are all men then derived from Adam?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He hath made of one blood all nations of men, (Acts 17:26)

**Q:** And did Adam himself derive his being from God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Who was the son of God, (Luke 3:38)

### Section 2

**Q:** Did God create man male and female?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Male and female created he them, (Genesis 5:2)

**Q:** Was there a difference in the manner of their creation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the man is not the woman, but the woman of the man, (1 Corinthians 11:8)

**Q:** And in the order of their creation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For Adam was first formed, and then Eve, (1 Timothy 2:13)

**Q:** And in the end of their creation?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the man was not created for the woman, but the woman for the man, (1 Corinthians 11:9)

### Section 3

**Q:** Was man made after the image of God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** God created man after his own image, (Genesis 1:27)

**Q:** Did that image consist in knowledge?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For we are renewed in knowledge after the image of him that created us, (Colossians 3:10)

**Q:** And in righteousness and holiness?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the new man after God, is created in righteousness and true holiness, (Ephesians 4:24)

**Q:** And in authority and government?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For thou hast put all things under his feet, (Psalm 8:6)

**Q:** Was this state of man a very glorious one?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou hast crowned him with glory and honour, (Psalm 8:5)

**Q:** But was it an abiding one?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** For man being in honour abideth not, (Psalm 49:12)
