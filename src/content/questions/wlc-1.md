title: What is the chief and highest end of man?
slug: wlc-1
categories:
  - wlc
---

Man’s chief and highest end is to glorify God, and fully to enjoy him forever.

## Proofs
- Man’s chief and highest end is to glorify God, (Romans 11:36; 1 Corinthians 10:31)
- and fully to enjoy him forever. (Psalm 73:24–28; John 17:21–23)
