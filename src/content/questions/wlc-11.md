title: How doth it appear that the Son and the Holy Ghost are God equal with the Father?
slug: wlc-11
categories:
  - wlc
---

The scriptures manifest that the Son and the Holy Ghost are God equal with the Father, ascribing unto them such names, attributes, works, and worship, as are proper to God only.

## Proofs
- The scriptures manifest that the Son and the Holy Ghost are God equal with the Father, ascribing unto them such names, (Isaiah 6:3, 5, 8; John 12:41; Acts 28:25; 1 John 5:20; Acts 5:3–4)
- attributes, (John 1:1; Isaiah 9:6; John 2:24–25; 1 Corinthians 2:10–11)
- works, (Colossians 1:16; Genesis 1:2)
- and worship, (Matthew 28:19; 2 Corinthians 13:14)
- as are proper to God only.
