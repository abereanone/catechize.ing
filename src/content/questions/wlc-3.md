The holy scriptures of the Old and New Testaments are the word of God, the only rule of faith and obedience.

## Proofs
- The holy scriptures of the Old and New Testaments are the word of God, (2 Timothy 3:16; 2 Peter 1:19–21)
- the only rule of faith and obedience. (Ephesians 2:20; Revelation 22:18–19; Isaiah 8:20; Luke 16:29, 31; Galatians 1:8–9; 2 Timothy 3:15–16)
