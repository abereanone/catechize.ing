Christ’s humiliation after his death consisted in his being buried, and continuing in the state of the dead, and under the power of death till the third day; which hath been otherwise expressed in these words, he descended into hell.

## Proofs
- Christ’s humiliation after his death consisted in his being buried, (1 Corinthians 15:3–4)
- and continuing in the state of the dead, and under the power of death till the third day; (Psalm 16:10; Acts 2:24–27, 31; Romans 6:9; Matthew 12:40)
- which hath been otherwise expressed in these words, he descended into hell.
