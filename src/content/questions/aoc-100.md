Those only which are done by a true Faith, according to God’s Law, and are referred only to his Glory, and not those which are imagined by us as seeming to us to be right and good, or which are delivered and commanded by Men.

## Proofs
- Those only which are done by a (Romans 14:23)
- true Faith, according (1 Samuel 15:22; Ephesians 2:10)
- to God’s Law, and are (1 Corinthians 10:31)
- referred only to his Glory, and not those which are imagined by us as seeming to us to be right and good, or which (Deuteronomy 11:32; Ezekiel 20:18–19; Isaiah 29:13; Matthew 15:9)
- are delivered and commanded by Men.
