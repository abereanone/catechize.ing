title: What are the parts of a sacrament?
slug: wlc-163
categories:
  - wlc
---

The parts of a sacrament are two; the one an outward and sensible sign, used according to Christ’s own appointment; the other an inward and spiritual grace thereby signified.

## Proofs
- The parts of a sacrament are two; the one an outward and sensible sign, used according to Christ’s own appointment; the other an inward and spiritual grace thereby signified. (Matthew 3:11; 1 Peter 3:21; Romans 2:28–29)
