For Thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever. That is: All this we ask of Thee because, as our King, having power over all things, Thou art both willing and able to give us all that is good, and because not we but Thy holy Name should so receive all glory for ever.

## Proofs
- For Thine is the kingdom, and the power, and the glory, for ever. That is: All this we ask of Thee because, as our King, having power over all things, Thou art both willing and able to give us all that is good, (Romans 10:11–13; 2 Peter 2:9)
- and because not we but Thy holy Name should so receive all glory for ever. (Psalm 115:1; Jeremiah 33:8–9; John 14:13)
