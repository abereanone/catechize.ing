The estate of Christ’s exaltation comprehendeth his resurrection, ascension, sitting at the right hand of the Father, and his coming again to judge the world.

## Proofs
- The estate of Christ’s exaltation comprehendeth his resurrection, (1 Corinthians 15:4)
- ascension, (Mark 16:19)
- sitting at the right hand of the Father, (Ephesians 1:20)
- and his coming again to judge the world. (Acts 1:11; 17:31)
