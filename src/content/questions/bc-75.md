The seventh commandment is, Thou shalt not commit adultery.

## Proofs
- The seventh commandment is, Thou shalt not commit adultery. (Exodus 20:14)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is adultery a sin which some are much inclined to?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They were as fed horses, every one neighing after his neighbour’s wife, (Jeremiah 5:8)

**Q:** Is it necessary therefore that it should be forbidden?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The law was made for whoremongers, (1 Timothy 1:10)

**Q:** Was it forbidden under the Old Testament?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou shalt not lie with thy neighbour’s wife to defile thyself with her, (Leviticus 18:20)

**Q:** And is it forbidden under the New?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For this is the will of God, that ye should abstain from fornication, (1 Thessalonians 4:3)

**Q:** And is it contrary to the law of nature?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For Abimelech calls adultery a great sin, (Genesis 20:9)
