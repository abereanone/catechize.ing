---
id: 21
title: Canons of Dort, 2nd Head, Article III - Of the death of Christ, and the redemption of men thereby
slug: cd-21
categories:
  - cd
---

The death of the Son of God is the only and most perfect sacrifice and satisfaction for sin; is of infinite worth and value, abundantly sufficient to expiate the sins of the whole world.

*Source: Public-domain 1840 translation from Wikisource (The Constitution of the Reformed Dutch Church of North America, "Canons of the Synod of Dordrecht").*
