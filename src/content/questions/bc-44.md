The duty which God requireth of man is, obedience to his revealed will.

## Proofs
- The duty which God requireth of man is, obedience to his revealed will. (Micah 6:8; 1 Samuel 15:22)

## Additional Exposition

<a href="https://baptistcatechism.org/beddome/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">From Benjamin Beddome</a>


### Section 1

**Q:** Hath God revealed his will?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He hath showed his word unto Jacob, his statutes and his judgments unto Israel, (Psalm 147:19)

**Q:** Hath he revealed it plainly?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** So that he may run which readeth it, (Habakkuk 2:2)

**Q:** And fully?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** I have not shunned to declare to you the whole counsel of God, (Acts 20:27)

**Q:** Should we therefore seek to be acquainted with it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Lead me in thy truth and teach me, (Psalm 25:5)

### Section 2

**Q:** Is obedience to God’s revealed will a duty?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** And now, O Israel, what doth the Lord thy God require of thee, but to walk in his ways, (Deuteronomy 10:12)

**Q:** Is it the duty of all?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Give ear all ye inhabitants of the world, both low and high, rich and poor, (Psalm 49:1–2)

**Q:** But especially of the redeemed?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Ye are bought with a price, therefore glorify God in your body, and in your spirit, which are God’s, (1 Corinthians 6:20)

**Q:** And is it their whole duty?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Let us hear the conclusion of the matter, fear God and keep his commandments, for this is the whole duty of man, (Ecclesiastes 12:13)

### Section 3

**Q:** Is it reasonable that God should be obeyed?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** It is our reasonable service, (Romans 12:1)

**Q:** Is obedience due to him as our Creator?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou art my servant, I have formed thee, (Isaiah 44:21)

**Q:** And as our preserver?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Is he not thy Father that hath bought thee, hath he not made and established thee, (Deuteronomy 32:6)

**Q:** And as our Master?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** If I be a Master, where is my fear? (Malachi 1:6)

**Q:** And as our Sovereign?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** All the ends of the earth shall remember and turn unto the Lord, for the kingdom is the Lord’s, (Psalm 22:27–28)

**Q:** Should God’s commands therefore take place of men’s?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Whether it be right in the sight of God to hearken unto you, more than unto God, judge ye, (Acts 4:19)
