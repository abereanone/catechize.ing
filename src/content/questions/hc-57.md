Not only shall my soul after this life immediately be taken up to Christ, my Head, but also this my flesh, raised by the power of Christ, shall be reunited with my soul and made like Christ’s glorious body.

## Proofs
- Not only shall my soul after this life immediately be taken up to Christ, my Head, (Luke 16:22; Luke 23:43; Philippians 1:21–23)
- but also this my flesh, raised by the power of Christ, shall be reunited with my soul and made like Christ’s glorious body. (Job 19:25–26; 1 Corinthians 15:20; 1 Corinthians 15:42–46; 1 Corinthians 15:54; Philippians 3:21; 1 John 3:2)
