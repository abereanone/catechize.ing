The preaching of the holy gospel and church discipline. By these two the kingdom of heaven is opened to believers and closed to unbelievers.

## Proofs
- The preaching of the holy gospel and church discipline. By these two the kingdom of heaven is opened to believers and closed to unbelievers. (Matthew 16:19; John 20:21–23)
