There is but one only, the living and true God.

## Proofs
- There is but one only, the living and true God. (Deuteronomy 6:4; Jeremiah 10:10)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is God the living God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He is the living God, (Daniel 6:26)

**Q:** Hath he life in himself?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The Father hath life in himself, (John 5:26)

**Q:** Doth he give life to others?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He quickeneth all things, (1 Timothy 6:13)

**Q:** Should living men therefore seek to the living God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Should not a people seek unto their God? for the living to the dead? (Isaiah 8:19)

### Section 2

**Q:** Is God the true God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** This is the true God, (1 John 5:20)

**Q:** Is he the only true God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** This is life eternal, to know thee the only true God, (John 17:3)

**Q:** And are all other gods false gods?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They are vanity and the work of errors, (Jeremiah 10:15)

### Section 3

**Q:** Is that God whom we serve, this only living and true God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The Lord is the true God, he is the living God, and an everlasting King, (Jeremiah 10:10)

**Q:** And is he but one?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Hear, O Israel, the Lord our God is one Lord, (Deuteronomy 6:4)

**Q:** Does God assume this prerogative to himself?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Is there a God besides me? Yea, there is no God; I know not any, (Isaiah 44:8)

**Q:** And have his people always ascribed it to him?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Unto us there is but one God, (1 Corinthians 8:6)

**Q:** But do not those who assert the deity of Christ destroy the unity of the godhead?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** For he says, I and my Father are one, (John 10:30)

### Section 4

**Q:** Are there many gods in name?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** There are gods many, and lords many, (1 Corinthians 8:5)

**Q:** But are these gods by nature?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** When ye knew not God, ye did service to them which by nature are no gods, (Galatians 4:8)

**Q:** Are they then to be reputed of?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** We know that an idol is nothing in the world, (1 Corinthians 8:4)

**Q:** Are those guilty of the greatest absurdity who worship them?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Those that make them are like unto them, and (Psalm 115:8)

**Q:** Do we well therefore not to imitate them?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Little children, keep yourselves from idols, (1 John 5:21)
