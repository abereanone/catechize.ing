title: Canons of Dort, 2nd Head, Article VII - Of the death of Christ, and the redemption of men thereby
slug: cd-25
categories:
  - cd
---

But as many as truly believe, and are delivered and saved from sin and destruction through the death of Christ, are indebted for this benefit solely to the grace of God given them in Christ from everlasting, and not to any merit of their own.

*Source: Public-domain 1840 translation from Wikisource (The Constitution of the Reformed Dutch Church of North America, "Canons of the Synod of Dordrecht").*
