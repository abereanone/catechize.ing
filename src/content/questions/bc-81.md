## Proofs
- The ninth commandment is, Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbour. (Exodus 20:16)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Is a good name a great privilege?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** ’Tis better than precious ointment, (Ecclesiastes 7:1)

**Q:** Is there a law then made for the security of it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The law is made for perjured persons, (1 Timothy 1:10)

**Q:** And is it needful that there should?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For every neighbour will walk with slanders, (Isaiah 4)
