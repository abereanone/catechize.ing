Sin is any want of conformity unto, or transgression of, any law of God, given as a rule to the reasonable creature.

## Proofs
- Sin is any want of conformity unto, or transgression of, any law of God, given as a rule to the reasonable creature. (1 John 3:4; Galatians 3:10, 12)
