title: Why is there added, Who art in heaven?
slug: hc-121
categories:
  - hc
---

These words teach us not to think of God’s heavenly majesty in an earthly manner, and to expect from His almighty power all things we need for body and soul.

## Proofs
- These words teach us not to think of God’s heavenly majesty in an earthly manner, (Jeremiah 23:23–24; Acts 17:24–25)
- and to expect from His almighty power all things we need for body and soul. (Matthew 6:25–34; Romans 8:31–32)
