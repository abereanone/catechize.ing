The first commandment forbiddeth the denying, or not worshipping and glorifying the true God, as God and our God, and the giving of that worship and glory to any other, which is due unto him alone.

## Proofs
- The first commandment forbiddeth the denying, (Psalm 14:1)
- or not worshipping and glorifying the true God, (Romans 1:21)
- as God and our God, (Psalm 81:10–11)
- and the giving of that worship and glory to any other, which is due unto him alone. (Romans 1:25–26)
