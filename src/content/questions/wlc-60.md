title: Can they who have never heard the gospel, and so know not Jesus Christ, nor believe in him, be saved by their living according to the light of nature?
slug: wlc-60
categories:
  - wlc
---

They who, having never heard the gospel, know not Jesus Christ, and believe not in him, cannot be saved, be they never so diligent to frame their lives according to the light of nature, or the laws of that religion which they profess; neither is there salvation in any other, but in Christ alone, who is the Savior only of his body the church.

## Proofs
- They who, having never heard the gospel, (Romans 10:14)
- know not Jesus Christ, (2 Thessalonians 1:8–9; Ephesians 2:12; John 1:10–12)
- and believe not in him, cannot be saved, (John 8:24; Mark 16:16)
- be they never so diligent to frame their lives according to the light of nature, (1 Corinthians 1:20–24)
- or the laws of that religion which they profess; (John 4:22; Romans 9:31–32; Philippians 3:4–9)
- neither is there salvation in any other, but in Christ alone, (Acts 4:12)
- who is the Savior only of his body the church. (Ephesians 5:23)
