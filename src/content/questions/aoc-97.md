It consisteth of the mortifying of the old Man, and a quickning of the new Man.

## Proofs
- It consisteth of the (Romans 6:4–6; Ephesians 4:22–24; Colossians 3:5,8–10; 1 Corinthians 5:7; 2 Corinthians 7:11)
- mortifying of the old Man, and a quickning of the new Man.
