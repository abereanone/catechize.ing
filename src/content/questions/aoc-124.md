But in forbidding Murther, God doth further teach, that he hateth the Root, to wit, Anger, Envy, Hatred, and Desire of Revenge, and doth account them all for Murther.

## Proofs
- But in forbidding Murther, God doth further teach, that he hateth the Root, to wit, (James 1:20; Galatians 5:20–21)
- Anger, (Romans 1:29)
- Envy, (1 John 2:9,11)
- Hatred, and Desire of Revenge, and (1 John 3:15)
- doth account them all for Murther.
