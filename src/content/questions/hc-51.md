---
id: 51
title: How does the glory of Christ, our Head, benefit us?
slug: hc-51
categories:
  - hc
---

First, by His Holy Spirit He pours out heavenly gifts upon us, His members. Second, by His power He defends and preserves us against all enemies.

## Proofs
- First, by His Holy Spirit He pours out heavenly gifts upon us, His members. (Acts 2:33; Ephesians 4:7–12)
- Second, by His power He defends and preserves us against all enemies. (Psalm 2:9; Psalm 110:1–2; John 10:27–30; Revelation 19:11–16)
