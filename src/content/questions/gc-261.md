title: "Shall we then dare to go to God familiarly, as a child to his father?"
slug: gc-261
categories:
  - gc
---

Yes, in fact with greater assurance of obtaining what we ask. For if we, being evil, cannot refuse our children bread and meat, when they ask, how much less will our heavenly Father, who is not only good, but sovereign goodness itself (Mat 7:11).
