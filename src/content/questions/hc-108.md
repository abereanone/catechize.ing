That all unchastity is cursed by God. We must therefore detest it from the heart and live chaste and disciplined lives, both within and outside of holy marriage.

## Proofs
- That all unchastity is cursed by God. (Leviticus 18:30; Ephesians 5:3–5)
- We must therefore detest it from the heart (Jude 1:22–23)
- and live chaste and disciplined lives, both within and outside of holy marriage. (1 Corinthians 7:1–9; 1 Thessalonians 4:3–8; Hebrews 13:4)
