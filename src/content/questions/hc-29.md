---
id: 29
title: Why is the Son of God called Jesus, that is, Saviour?
slug: hc-29
categories:
  - hc
---

Because He saves us from all our sins, and because salvation is not to be sought or found in anyone else.

## Proofs
- Because He saves us from all our sins, (Matthew 1:21; Hebrews 7:25)
- and because salvation is not to be sought or found in anyone else. (Isaiah 43:11; John 15:4–5; Acts 4:11–12; 1 Timothy 2:5)
