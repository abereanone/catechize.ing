---
id: 350
title: "The Supper, then, was not instituted in order to offer up the body of Jesus the Son to the Father?"
slug: gc-350
categories:
  - gc
---

No, for this office pertains to none but Him alone, since He is the eternal Priest (Heb. 5:5). But He commands us only to receive His body, not to offer it (Mat 26:26).
