Christ, by his mediation, hath procured redemption, with all other benefits of the covenant of grace.

## Proofs
- Christ, by his mediation, hath procured redemption, (Hebrews 9:12)
- with all other benefits of the covenant of grace. (2 Corinthians 1:20)
