---
id: 33
title: Why is He called God’s only begotten Son, since we also are children of God?
slug: hc-33
categories:
  - hc
---

Because Christ alone is the eternal, natural Son of God. We, however, are children of God by adoption, through grace, for Christ’s sake.

## Proofs
- Because Christ alone is the eternal, natural Son of God. (John 1:1–3; John 1:14; John 1:18; John 3:16; Romans 8:32; Hebrews 1; 1 John 4:9)
- We, however, are children of God by adoption, through grace, for Christ’s sake. (John 1:12; Romans 8:14–17; Galatians 4:6; Ephesians 1:5–6)
