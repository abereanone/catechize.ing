That Christ, his Disciples looking on, was taken up from the Earth into Heaven, and yet still is there for our sakes, and will be until he come again to judge the quick and the dead.

## Proofs
- That Christ, his Disciples looking on, was (Acts 1:9; Mark 16:19; Luke 24:51)
- taken up from the Earth into Heaven, and yet (Hebrews 4:14; Hebrews 7:25; Hebrews 9:11; Romans 8:34; Ephesians 4:10; Colossians 3:1)
- still is there for our sakes, and will be until he come again to judge the quick and (Acts 1:11; Matthew 24:30)
- the dead.
