And forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors. That is: For the sake of Christ’s blood, do not impute to us, wretched sinners; any of our transgressions, nor the evil which still clings to us, as we also find this evidence of Thy grace in us that we are fully determined wholeheartedly to forgive our neighbor.

## Proofs
- And forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors. That is: For the sake of Christ’s blood, do not impute to us, wretched sinners; any of our transgressions, nor the evil which still clings to us, (Psalm 51:1–7; Psalm 143:2; Romans 8:1; 1 John 2:1–2)
- as we also find this evidence of Thy grace in us that we are fully determined wholeheartedly to forgive our neighbor. (Matthew 6:14–15; Matthew 18:21–35)
