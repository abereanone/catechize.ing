Everyone ought to believe there is a God; and it is their great sin and folly who do not.

## Proofs
- Everyone ought to believe there is a God; (Hebrews 11:6)
- and it is their great sin and folly who do not. (Psalm 14:1)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Are there any that disbelieve the being of a God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They say in their heart there is no God, (Psalm 14:1)

**Q:** And does this open a door to all immoralities?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They are corrupt, they have done abominable works, (Psalm 14:1)

**Q:** Is the being of God then a necessary article of the christian faith?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He that cometh to God must believe that he is (Hebrews 11:6)

**Q:** And is it the foundation of all practical religion?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** How shall they call on him in whom they have not believed, (Romans 10:14)

**Q:** Is God’s existence a comfortable thought to the saint?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Verily, there is a God that judgeth in the earth, (Psalm 58:11)

**Q:** And an awful thought to the wicked?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Fear ye not me, saith the Lord, (Jeremiah 5:22)

### God as first

**Q:** Did the first man believe there was a God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For he heard his voice and was afraid, (Genesis 3:10)

**Q:** Did other men in the first ages of the world believe it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For they called upon his name, (Genesis 4:26)

**Q:** Have not all nations believed it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They knew God, though they glorified him not as God, (Romans 1:21)

**Q:** Do holy spirits above believe there is a God?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They rest not day and night, saying, Holy, holy, holy, Lord God Almighty, (Revelation 4:8)

**Q:** Do the devils believe it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The devils believe and tremble, (James 2:19)

**Q:** Do we well then to believe it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou believest that there is one God; thou dost well, (James 2:19)

### Section 3

**Q:** Is ignorance of God a folly?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** My people are foolish, they have not known me, (Jeremiah 4:22)

**Q:** And a shame?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Some have not the knowledge of God, I speak it to your shame, (1 Corinthians 15:34)

**Q:** And a sin?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They have perverted their way, and forgotten the Lord their God, (Jeremiah 3:21)

**Q:** Is it an inexcusable sin?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** They are without excuse, (Romans 1:20)

**Q:** And a destructive sin?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He shall be driven from light into darkness, this is the place of him that knoweth not God, (Job 18:21)
