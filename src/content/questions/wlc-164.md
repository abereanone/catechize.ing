Under the New Testament Christ hath instituted in his church only two sacraments, Baptism and the Lord’s supper.

## Proofs
- Under the New Testament Christ hath instituted in his church only two sacraments, Baptism and the Lord’s supper. (Matthew 28:19; 1 Corinthians 11:20, 23; Matthew 26:26–28)
