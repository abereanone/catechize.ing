That we should not express or represent God by any Image or shape and figure, or worship him any otherwise then he hath commanded himself in his Word to be worshipped.

## Proofs
- That we should not (Deuteronomy 4:15,&c; Isaiah 40:18,&c; Romans 1:23,&c; Acts 17:29)
- express or represent God by any Image or shape and figure, or worship him any otherwise then he hath commanded himself in (1 Samuel 15:23; Deuteronomy 12:30,&c; Matthew 15:9)
- his Word to be worshipped.
