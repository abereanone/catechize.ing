title: Canons of Dort, 5th Head, Article VII - Of the Perseverance of the Saints
slug: cd-51

For in the first place, in these falls he preserves in them the incorruptible seed of regeneration from perishing, or being totally lost; and again, by his word and Spirit certainly and effectually renews them to repentance, to a sincere and godly sorrow for their sins, that they may seek and obtain remission in the blood of the Mediator, may again experience the favour of a reconciled God, through faith adore his mercies, and henceforward more diligently work out their own salvation with fear and trembling.

*Source: Public-domain 1840 translation from Wikisource (The Constitution of the Reformed Dutch Church of North America, "Canons of the Synod of Dordrecht").*
