The work of creation is God’s making all things of nothing, by the word of his power, in the space of six days, and all very good.

## Proofs
- The work of creation is God’s making all things of nothing, by the word of his power, in the space of six days, and all very good. (Genesis 1 throughout; Hebrews 11:3)

## Additional Exposition

### Section 1

**Q:** Did God create the world?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Thou, Lord, in the beginning hast laid the foundation of the earth, and the heavens are the works of thine hands, (Hebrews 1:10)

**Q:** And all things in it?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** God made the world and all things therein, (Acts 17:24)

**Q:** Did he create the soul of man?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Who formeth the spirit of man within him, (Zechariah 12:1)

**Q:** And the body of man?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** In thy book were all my members written, (Psalm 139:16)

**Q:** Did he create the highest angels?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** By him were all things created, whether they be thrones, etc., (Colossians 1:16)

**Q:** And the meanest insects?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** God made every thing that creepeth upon the earth after his kind, (Genesis 1:25)

**Q:** And what omnipotence wrought, did omniscience approve?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** God saw every thing that he had made and behold it was very good, (Genesis 1:31)

### Section 2

**Q:** Did God need any assistance in his work?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** Who hath prevented me, that I should repay him? (Job 41:11)

**Q:** Did he make all things out of nothing?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For the things which are seen were not made of the things that do appear, (Hebrews 11:3)

**Q:** And by his word?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** He said let there be light: and there was light, (Genesis 1:3)

**Q:** And in six days?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** In six days the Lord made heaven and earth, (Exodus 20:11)

**Q:** Did he appoint every creature its proper station and use?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Let there be lights in the firmament, and let them be for signs and for seasons, for days and for years, (Genesis 1:14)

**Q:** Does God go on to create?  
**A:** No.  
**Proof:** On the seventh day God rested from all his works, (Genesis 2:2)
