title: Why did He suffer under Pontius Pilate as judge?
slug: hc-38
categories:
  - hc

Though innocent, Christ was condemned by an earthly judge, and so He freed us from the severe judgment of God that was to fall on us.

## Proofs
- Though innocent, Christ was condemned by an earthly judge, (Luke 23:13–24; John 19:4; John 19:12–16)
- and so He freed us from the severe judgment of God that was to fall on us. (Isaiah 53:4–5; 2 Corinthians 5:21; Galatians 3:13)
