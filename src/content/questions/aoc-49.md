Because Christ therefore is ascended into Heaven, to shew there that he is the Head of his Church, by whom the Father governeth all things.

## Proofs
- Because Christ therefore is ascended into Heaven, to (Ephesians 1:20; Ephesians 5:23; Colossians 1:18)
- shew there that he is the Head of his Church, by whom the Father (Matthew 28:18; John 5:22)
- governeth all things.
