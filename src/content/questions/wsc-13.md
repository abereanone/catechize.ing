Our first parents, being left to the freedom of their own will, fell from the estate wherein they were created, by sinning against God.

## Proofs
- Our first parents, being left to the freedom of their own will, fell from the estate wherein they were created, by sinning against God. (Genesis 3:6–8; Genesis 3:13; Ecclesiastes 7:29)
