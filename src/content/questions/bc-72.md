## Additional Exposition

<a href="https://baptistcatechism.org/beddome/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">From Benjamin Beddome</a>


### Section 1

**Q:** Are the lives of men in their own nature precious?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** The life is more than meat, (Matthew 6:25)

**Q:** Hath God therefore set a hedge round about them?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** Of every man will I require the life of man, (Genesis 9:5)

**Q:** And was it needful that he should do so?  
**A:** Yes.  
**Proof:** For out of the heart proceed murders, (Matthew 15:19)
