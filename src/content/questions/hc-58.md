Since I now already feel in my heart the beginning of eternal joy, I shall after this life possess perfect blessedness, such as no eye has seen, nor ear heard, nor the heart of man conceived—a blessedness in which to praise God forever.

## Proofs
- Since I now already feel in my heart the beginning of eternal joy, (John 17:3; Romans 14:17; 2 Corinthians 5:2–3)
- I shall after this life possess perfect blessedness, such as no eye has seen, nor ear heard, nor the heart of man conceived—a blessedness in which to praise God forever. (John 17:24; 1 Corinthians 2:9)
