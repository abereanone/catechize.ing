slug: wlc-131

The duties of equals are, to regard the dignity and worth of each other, in giving honor to go one before another; and to rejoice in each other’s gifts and advancement, as their own.

## Proofs
- The duties of equals are, to regard the dignity and worth of each other, (1 Peter 2:17)
- in giving honor to go one before another; (Romans 12:10)
- and to rejoice in each other’s gifts and advancement, as their own. (Romans 12:15–16; Philippians 2:3–4)
