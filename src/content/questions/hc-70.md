To be washed with Christ’s blood means to receive forgiveness of sins from God, through grace, because of Christ’s blood, poured out for us in His sacrifice on the cross. To be washed with His Spirit means to be renewed by the Holy Spirit and sanctified to be members of Christ, so that more and more we become dead to sin and lead a holy and blameless life.

## Proofs
- To be washed with Christ’s blood means to receive forgiveness of sins from God, through grace, because of Christ’s blood, poured out for us in His sacrifice on the cross. (Ezekiel 36:25; Zechariah 13:1; Ephesians 1:7; Hebrews 12:24; 1 Peter 1:2; Revelation 1:5; Revelation 7:14)
- To be washed with His Spirit means to be renewed by the Holy Spirit and sanctified to be members of Christ, so that more and more we become dead to sin and lead a holy and blameless life. (John 3:5–8; Romans 6:4; 1 Corinthians 6:11; Colossians 2:11–12)
