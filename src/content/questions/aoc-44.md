First, by his Resurrection he vanquished death, that he might make us partakers of that Righteousness which he had gotten us by his death. Again, we are now also stirred up by his Power to a new Life. Lastly, the Resurrection of our head, Christ, is a Pledge unto us of our glorious Resurrection.

## Proofs
- First, by his Resurrection he vanquished death, that he (1 Corinthians 15:6; 1 Corinthians 5:4–6; Romans 4:25; 1 Peter 1:3,&c and 1Pe 1:21)
- might make us partakers of that Righteousness which he had gotten us by his death. Again, we are now (Romans 6:4; Colossians 3:1,&c; Ephesians 2:5)
- also stirred up by his Power to a new Life. Lastly, the Resurrection of our head, Christ, is a (1 Corinthians 15:12,&c; Romans 8:11)
- Pledge unto us of our glorious Resurrection.
